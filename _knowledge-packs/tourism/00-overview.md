# Tourism & Hospitality — Knowledge Pack

## Sector Overview

Hotels, lodges, tour operators, and travel services

## Zimbabwe Context

Zimbabwe's tourism sector is anchored by Victoria Falls, Hwange National Park, and Great Zimbabwe, and is a key forex earner regulated by the Zimbabwe Tourism Authority (ZTA). The UNIVISA (KAZA) scheme with Zambia simplifies cross-border travel. Key challenges: seasonal demand swings, USD-pricing in a dual-currency economy, and building direct-booking channels to reduce dependence on foreign OTAs.

## Key Considerations for Zimbabwe Operations

- Dual-currency environment (USD/ZWL) requires careful financial handling
- Mobile-first strategy recommended given high smartphone penetration
- Internet connectivity varies between urban and rural areas

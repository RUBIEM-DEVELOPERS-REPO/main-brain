# HealthTech & Public Health — Knowledge Pack

## Sector Overview

Healthcare delivery, patient records, telemedicine, and public health monitoring

## Zimbabwe Context

Zimbabwe's health system faces challenges with infectious diseases (HIV/TB/malaria), maternal health, and a growing NCD burden. The MOHCC (Ministry of Health) drives digital health through DHIS2. Key opportunities: stock-out prediction for essential medicines, patient journey tracking, and community health worker coordination.

## Key Considerations for Zimbabwe Operations
- Regulatory compliance is critical — ensure all automated workflows align with local laws
- Dual-currency environment (USD/ZWL) requires careful financial handling
- Mobile-first strategy recommended given high smartphone penetration
- Internet connectivity varies between urban and rural areas

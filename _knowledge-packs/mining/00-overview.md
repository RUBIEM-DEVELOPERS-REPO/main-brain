# Mining & Resources — Knowledge Pack

## Sector Overview

Mining operations, mineral tracking, safety compliance, and supply chain

## Zimbabwe Context

Zimbabwe is rich in minerals: gold (the largest producer after SA), platinum group metals (Zimplats, Mimosa, Unki), diamonds (Marange), lithium (Arcadian, Kamativi), and chrome. The mining sector is a major foreign currency earner. Key challenges: artisanal mining regulation, royalty compliance (ZIMRA), and community development agreements.

## Key Considerations for Zimbabwe Operations
- Regulatory compliance is critical — ensure all automated workflows align with local laws
- Dual-currency environment (USD/ZWL) requires careful financial handling
- Mobile-first strategy recommended given high smartphone penetration
- Internet connectivity varies between urban and rural areas

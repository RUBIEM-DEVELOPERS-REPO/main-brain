# IT & Tech — Knowledge Pack

## Sector Overview

Software, telecoms, digital services, and the local start-up ecosystem

## Zimbabwe Context

Zimbabwe's tech sector is regulated by POTRAZ (telecoms/postal) and anchored by players like Econet/Cassava and a growing fintech-adjacent start-up scene. Mobile penetration outpaces fixed broadband, so mobile-first product design matters. Key challenges: forex access for cloud/SaaS spend, data protection compliance under the Data Protection Act, and load-shedding-resilient infrastructure.

## Key Considerations for Zimbabwe Operations
- Regulatory compliance is critical — ensure all automated workflows align with local laws
- Dual-currency environment (USD/ZWL) requires careful financial handling
- Mobile-first strategy recommended given high smartphone penetration
- Internet connectivity varies between urban and rural areas

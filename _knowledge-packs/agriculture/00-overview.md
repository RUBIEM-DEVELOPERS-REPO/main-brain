# Agriculture & Agribusiness — Knowledge Pack

## Sector Overview

Farming operations, supply chain, crop monitoring, and market access

## Zimbabwe Context

Agriculture employs ~70% of Zimbabwe's workforce. Key challenges: climate-smart practices, input financing, market linkages, and extension services. The Pfumvudza/Intwasa program is a major government initiative for smallholder farmers.

## Key Considerations for Zimbabwe Operations
- Regulatory compliance is critical — ensure all automated workflows align with local laws
- Dual-currency environment (USD/ZWL) requires careful financial handling
- Mobile-first strategy recommended given high smartphone penetration
- Internet connectivity varies between urban and rural areas

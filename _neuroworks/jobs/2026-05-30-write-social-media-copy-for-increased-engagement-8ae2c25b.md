## Social Media Copy Strategy for Higher Engagement  

**Objective** – Generate more likes, comments, shares, and click‑throughs across the brand’s primary channels (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter) while reinforcing the brand voice and key messaging.

### Core Tactics  
- **Use a hook in the first 3–5 words** to stop scrolling (e.g., “Ready to level‑up?”).  
- **Add a clear call‑to‑action** that tells the audience exactly what to do (e.g., “Tap to claim your free trial”).  
- **Incorporate visual cues** such as emojis or line breaks to improve readability on mobile feeds.  
- **Leverage user‑generated content** by prompting followers to share their own experiences with a branded hashtag.  
- **Tie posts to timely events** (product launches, holidays, industry news) to increase relevance.

### Sample Copy  

| Platform | Copy (≈ 150 characters) |
|----------|--------------------------|
| **Facebook** | 🎉 Ready to level‑up your workflow? Discover how our AI‑assistant saves you 3 hrs/week. 👉 Click to start your free 14‑day trial! #ProductivityBoost |
| **Instagram** | ✨ Transform the way you work in just minutes. Swipe up for a free demo and join the #FutureOfWork community! |
| **LinkedIn** | 🚀 Companies that adopt our solution see a 27 % rise in efficiency within 30 days. Learn how you can stay ahead—download the case study now. |
| **Twitter** | Want more time for strategy, not admin? Our tool cuts tasks in half. Try it free → [link] #WorkSmarter |

### Measurement  
- Track **engagement rate** (likes + comments + shares ÷ impressions) weekly.  
- Monitor **click‑through rate** on CTA links.  
- Use A/B testing on hooks and emojis to refine tone.

*Assumed: the brand offers a productivity‑focused AI tool; the audience consists of professionals and small‑business owners.*

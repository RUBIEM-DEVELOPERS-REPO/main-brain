| Time | Activity | Purpose | Key Artefacts / Tools |
|------|----------|---------|-----------------------|

| **10:00 – 10:30** | **Feature Request Intake** | Log new feature requests or bugs from customers; triage with Product on impact to NRR. | Product‑feedback portal, JIRA |
| **10:30 – 11:00** | **Save‑Play Preparation** | For any account flagged as at‑risk, draft a “save play” (action plan, owners, timeline). | Playbook template, shared doc |
| **11:00 – 12:00** | **Internal Sync** | Quick stand‑up with the CS Ops team to align on priorities and share insights. | Teams meeting, shared agenda |
| **12:00 – 13:00** | **Lunch / Buffer** | – | – |

| **15:00 – 16:00** | **Product Collaboration** | Attend Product backlog grooming to advocate for high‑impact feature requests. | Product meeting, backlog |
| **16:00 – 17:00** | **Documentation & Reporting** | Update playbooks, QBR decks, and NRR reports; archive decisions. | Confluence, Google Drive |

### Weekly Cadence

| Day | Activity | Outcome | Tools |
|-----|----------|---------|-------|
| **Monday** | **QBR Deck Finalization** | Compile usage, health, NRR, and feature‑request status for the 30 accounts. | PowerPoint, analytics export |
| **Tuesday** | **Internal QBR Review** | Review deck with CS Ops and Product; refine talking points. | Teams, shared deck |
| **Wednesday** | **Client QBR Delivery** | Conduct 1‑hour QBR with each account (rotating schedule). | Zoom, QBR deck, meeting notes |
| **Thursday** | **Post‑QBR Follow‑Up** | Capture action items, send thank‑you notes, update NRR forecast. | CRM, email |
| **Friday** | **Save‑Play Execution & Review** | Execute at‑risk account playbooks; debrief with owners; update risk register. | Playbook, shared spreadsheet |

### Save‑Play Loop (At‑Risk Accounts)

1. **Trigger** – Health score < 70 % or usage drop > 20 %.  
2. **Impact Scoping** – Estimate potential churn loss and NRR impact.  
3. **Owner Assignment** – Assign CS Manager, Product Lead, and Support Lead.  
4. **Action Plan** – Define 3‑step play (e.g., executive brief, feature demo, renewal incentive).  
5. **Execution** – Coordinate calls, demos, and contract negotiations.  
6. **Status Update** – Daily stand‑up on play progress.  
7. **Escalation** – If no progress after 3 days, involve VP of Customer Success.  
8. **Post‑Mortem** – Document lessons, update playbook.

### Key Metrics Tracked Daily / Weekly

- **Net Revenue Retention (NRR)** – % change vs. prior period.  
- **Churn Rate** – % of accounts lost.  
- **At‑Risk Account Count** – number of accounts flagged.  
- **Feature Request Velocity** – number of requests logged vs. resolved.  
- **QBR Satisfaction Score** – client feedback post‑meeting.

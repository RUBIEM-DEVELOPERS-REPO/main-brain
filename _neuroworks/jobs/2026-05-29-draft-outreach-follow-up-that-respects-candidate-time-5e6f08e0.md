## Outreach & Follow‑up Templates for Talent Recruiters  
*Designed to be concise, respectful of the candidate’s time, and easy to personalize.*

### 1️⃣ Initial Outreach (≈ 90 words)

**Subject:** Quick note about a role that fits your background  

Hi [First Name],

I’m [Your Name] from [Company]. I was impressed by your work on [Specific achievement] and think you’d be a strong fit for our [Role Title]. I’d love to share how the position aligns with your experience in a brief 10‑minute call. If you’re interested, could you let me know a convenient time this week?

Thanks for your time,  
[Your Name] | [Phone] | [LinkedIn]

---

### 2️⃣ First Follow‑up (2–3 days later, ≈ 60 words)

**Subject:** Quick follow‑up  

Hi [First Name],

Just checking whether you saw my previous message. I understand you’re busy, so a brief 10‑minute chat would be all I’m asking. Let me know a time that works for you.

Thanks,  
[Your Name]

---

### 3️⃣ Second Follow‑up (1 week later, ≈ 50 words)

**Subject:** Final note – still interested?  

Hi [First Name],

I don’t want to clutter your inbox, so this will be my last outreach. If you’re open to learning more, please reply; otherwise I’ll assume you’re not interested.

Appreciate your time,  
[Your Name]

---

### Key Practices  
- **Keep each message under 100 words.**  
- **State the time commitment upfront (10 min).**  
- **Use a clear, respectful cadence:** initial contact → first follow‑up → final follow‑up.  
- **Personalize with a specific achievement or skill** to demonstrate genuine interest.  
- **Avoid academic language or unnecessary citations.**

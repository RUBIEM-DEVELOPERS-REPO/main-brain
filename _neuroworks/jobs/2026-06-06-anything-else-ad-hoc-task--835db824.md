## Answer
Here are three signs indicating that a microservice might benefit from being merged back into a monolithic architecture:

- **High Dependency Complexity:** If the microservice has an excessive number of dependencies, making it tightly coupled with other services.
- **Data Consistency Issues:** Persistent data consistency problems across multiple services can be mitigated by consolidating them under a single service.
- **Increased Operational Overhead:** Frequent integration issues and increased operational complexity due to distributed systems challenges.

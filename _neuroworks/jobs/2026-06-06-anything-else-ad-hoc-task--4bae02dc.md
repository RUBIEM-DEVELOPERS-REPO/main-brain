## Answer
Feature Flags offer several key benefits that can be particularly advantageous for small teams:

1. **Incremental Rollouts**: They allow developers to roll out new features in stages, enabling the team to monitor and gather user feedback without affecting all users at once.
2. **Cost-Effective Testing**: Feature Flags facilitate testing of experimental or risky features by isolating them from the main application codebase, reducing potential disruptions if issues arise.
3. **Flexibility in Maintenance**: With feature flags, teams can easily enable or disable features as needed, making it easier to maintain and update their applications without complete recompilation or redeployment.

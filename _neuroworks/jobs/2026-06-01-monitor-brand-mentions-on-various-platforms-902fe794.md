## Monitoring Brand Mentions – A Quick Guide for Marketing Specialists  

### Core Platforms to Track  
- **Social Media** – Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok.  
- **Search Engines** – Google and Bing alerts for organic mentions.  
- **News & Blogs** – Google News, industry‑specific RSS feeds, and blog monitoring tools.  
- **Review Sites** – Trustpilot, G2, Capterra, Amazon (product reviews).  
- **Forums & Communities** – Reddit, Quora, niche industry forums, Discord channels.  

### Recommended Tool Stack  
| Category | Tool Examples | Key Features |
|---|---|---|
| Social Listening | Brandwatch, Sprout Social, Hootsuite Insights | Real‑time keyword tracking, sentiment analysis, influencer identification |
| Web & News Alerts | Google Alerts, Talkwalker Alerts | Email notifications for new web pages or news articles containing brand terms |
| Review Management | ReviewTrackers, Yotpo | Centralized dashboard for rating trends and response workflows |
| Forum Monitoring | Mention, Awario | Ability to crawl Reddit, Discord, and niche forums for brand keywords |
| Sentiment & Analytics | Lexalytics, MonkeyLearn | AI‑driven sentiment scoring and topic clustering across sources |

### Process Overview  
1. **Define Keywords** – Include brand name, product names, common misspellings, and relevant hashtags.  
2. **Set Up Alerts** – Configure each tool to push real‑time notifications to a shared Slack channel or email distribution list.  
3. **Categorize Mentions** – Tag incoming mentions by source, sentiment (positive, neutral, negative), and urgency.  
4. **Analyze Trends** – Generate weekly dashboards showing volume spikes, sentiment shifts, and top influencers.  
5. **Respond Strategically** – Draft response templates for common scenarios (e.g., customer complaints, misinformation) and assign ownership to team members.  

### Best Practices  
- **Regularly Review Keyword List** – Add emerging product names or campaign hashtags as they launch.  
- **Cross‑Validate Data** – Compare metrics from multiple tools to reduce false positives.  
- **Integrate with CRM** – Link brand‑mention data to customer records for a 360° view of interactions.  
- **Document SOPs** – Maintain a living playbook outlining escalation paths for negative mentions.

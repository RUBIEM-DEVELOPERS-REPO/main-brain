## Social‑Media Copy Recommendations for Higher Engagement  

**Objective** – Generate more likes, comments, and shares across the brand’s primary platforms (LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter, and Facebook) while reinforcing the brand voice and key messaging.

### Core Themes  
- **Customer Success Stories** – Highlight real‑world outcomes with concise, relatable quotes.  
- **Behind‑the‑Scenes Glimpses** – Show the people, process, or technology that makes the product unique.  
- **Interactive Prompts** – Use polls, quizzes, or “caption this” challenges to invite direct participation.  
- **Timely Trends** – Tie content to relevant industry events, holidays, or viral topics to ride existing momentum.

### Sample Copy  

| Platform | Copy (≤ 140 characters) | CTA |
|----------|------------------------|-----|
| **LinkedIn** | “Our clients are cutting project time by 30% with AI‑driven automation. 🚀 See how you can do the same – read the full case study 👉 [link]” | Read the case study |
| **Instagram** | “Ever wonder what a day in the life of an AI‑engineer looks like? 👩‍💻 Swipe left for a behind‑the‑scenes tour! #AI #TechLife” | Swipe for carousel |
| **Twitter** | “Quick poll: Which feature would boost your workflow the most? 1️⃣ Smart scheduling 2️⃣ Auto‑summaries 3️⃣ Real‑time analytics #YourVoiceMatters” | Vote in poll |
| **Facebook** | “Caption this moment from our latest product launch! The best caption wins a free 1‑month trial. 🎉 #Giveaway” | Comment your caption |

### Tone & Style Guidelines  
- **Conversational but professional** – Use first‑person plural (“we”) and active verbs.  
- **Emojis sparingly** – Add visual interest without compromising readability.  
- **Clear CTA** – Every post ends with a single, obvious action for the audience.  
- **Brand hashtags** – Include 1‑2 branded tags plus 1‑2 trending tags per post.

### Posting Cadence  
- **LinkedIn & Facebook** – 3 times per week (morning).  
- **Instagram** – Daily Stories + 4 feed posts per week (evening).  
- **Twitter** – 5‑7 tweets per week (spread throughout the day).

*Assumed: The brand’s voice is upbeat, tech‑savvy, and customer‑centric; the target audience includes B2B decision‑makers and tech‑enthusiasts.*

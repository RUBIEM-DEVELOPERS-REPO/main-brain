## Early‑Warning Red‑Flag Report for Talent Recruiters  

**Prepared by:** Clawbot – Talent Acquisition Support  
**Date:** 2026‑05‑29  

### 1. Skill Mismatch  
- **Resume/Interview Gaps:** Candidates omit core competencies required for the role (e.g., a data‑science applicant lacking statistical methods).  
- **Over‑qualification:** Senior‑level experience applied to junior openings may indicate a poor fit or hidden expectations.  

### 2. Compensation Gap  
- **Salary Expectation Discrepancy:** Requests that exceed the market range for the position or the company’s budget.  
- **Benefit Mismatch:** Candidates repeatedly negotiate for benefits outside the standard package, signalling potential future dissatisfaction.  

### 3. Motivation Drift  
- **Inconsistent Narrative:** Frequent changes in career goals or vague answers during interviews.  
- **Engagement Lapses:** Delayed email responses, lack of follow‑up after initial contact, or negative remarks about prior employers/projects.  

### 4. Recommended Early‑Warning Actions  
- **Validate Skills:** Deploy a brief technical assessment or conduct reference checks to confirm claimed abilities.  
- **Clarify Compensation Early:** Discuss salary expectations and benefits in the first interview to surface gaps promptly.  
- **Assess Motivation:** Use situational questions and monitor candidate engagement; follow up promptly to gauge interest.  

By systematically monitoring these indicators, recruiters can make informed decisions—whether to continue the process, adjust the offer, or redirect efforts to more suitable candidates.

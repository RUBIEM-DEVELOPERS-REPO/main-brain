## Monitoring Brand Mentions – Quick Guide for a Marketing Specialist  

### Core Platforms to Track  
- **Social Media:** Twitter/X, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, TikTok.  
- **Review Sites:** Google My Business, Trustpilot, Yelp, industry‑specific forums.  
- **News & Blogs:** Google News alerts, RSS feeds from relevant publications, blog comment sections.  
- **Video & Audio:** YouTube, podcasts, streaming platforms where brand names may appear.  

### Recommended Tools  
| Tool | Primary Strength | Typical Use Case |
|------|------------------|------------------|
| **Brandwatch / Talkwalker** | Real‑time social listening with sentiment analysis | High‑volume monitoring across multiple social channels |
| **Google Alerts** | Free, email‑based keyword alerts | Catching news articles, blog posts, and web mentions |
| **Mention** | Combines social, web, and forum tracking in one dashboard | Small‑to‑medium teams needing a unified view |
| **Hootsuite Insights** | Integrated with scheduling and reporting | Teams already using Hootsuite for social publishing |
| **BuzzSumo** | Content performance and backlink monitoring | Identifying viral mentions and influencer shares |

### Workflow Overview  
1. **Define Keywords** – Include brand name, common misspellings, product names, and relevant hashtags.  
2. **Set Up Alerts** – Configure each tool to push real‑time notifications to Slack, email, or a central dashboard.  
3. **Categorize Mentions** – Tag as *positive*, *neutral*, or *negative*; flag for response or escalation.  
4. **Analyze Sentiment** – Use built‑in sentiment scoring or manual review for nuanced cases.  
5. **Report Weekly** – Summarize volume, sentiment trends, top sources, and any emerging issues.  

### Best Practices  
- **Regularly Refresh Keywords** to capture new product launches or slang.  
- **Combine Automated Alerts with Manual Spot‑Checks** to catch false positives/negatives.  
- **Establish Response Protocols** so the community team can act quickly on negative mentions.  
- **Benchmark Against Competitors** to gauge relative brand health.

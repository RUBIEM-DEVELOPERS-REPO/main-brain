## Answer
**All‑Staff Announcement – New Expense Policy Effective 1 May 2026**

Dear Team,

Starting 1 May 2026 we are rolling out an updated Staff Expense Policy. The new rules are designed to give you clear, fair guidance on allowable costs while ensuring compliance with tax and regulatory requirements.

**Key financial limits**

* **Travel – Airfare:** Economy class only; up to $800 domestic, $2,500 international (any upgrade needs manager approval).  
* **Ground transportation:** Ride‑share or taxi ≤ $75 per trip; rental cars ≤ $60 per day (standard class). Mileage reimbursement is now **$0.68 per mile** (U.S.) or the local tax‑authority rate.  
* **Accommodation:** ≤ $180 per night (city‑average). Premium rooms require pre‑approval.  
* **Meals & Entertainment:** $55 per day (breakfast $15, lunch $20, dinner $20). Alcohol is excluded except for approved client entertainment, capped at $30 per person with a business‑purpose statement.  
* **Supplies & Small Equipment:** ≤ $150 per item; > $500 needs a procurement requisition.  
* **Professional Development:** Conference fees ≤ $1,200 per event; total travel + lodging ≤ $2,500 without senior‑lead approval.  

**Spending caps**

* Monthly spend per employee ≤ $5,000 (standard tier).  
* Senior staff and field roles have higher tiers that must be approved by senior management.  

**Reimbursement process**

* Submit expense reports within **15 business days** using the Expense Management System (EMS) with original, item‑ized receipts (digital copies accepted).  
* All expenses are recorded **pre‑tax**; VAT/GST must be shown on a tax‑compliant invoice to be recoverable.  
* Retain receipts for **seven years**; after that, dispose of them securely in line with GDPR and IRS guidelines.  

**Compliance safeguards**

* Country‑specific per‑diem tables will be applied where local regulations differ.  
* Random quarterly audits will verify adherence; non‑compliant items may be denied.  
* The policy will be reviewed annually to keep mileage rates, per‑diem limits, and tax rules up to date.  

The full policy document, including detailed workflow diagrams and FAQ, is available on the intranet under **HR → Policies → Expense Management**. Please read it carefully and direct any questions to the Finance team (Cole) or Compliance (Cora).

Thank you for your cooperation as we streamline expense handling and maintain fiscal responsibility.

Best regards,  
Piers  
Communications Manager

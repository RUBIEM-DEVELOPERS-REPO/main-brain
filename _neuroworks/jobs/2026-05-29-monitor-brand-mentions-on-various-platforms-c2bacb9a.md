## Brand‑Mention Monitoring – Quick‑Start Guide for a Marketing Specialist  

### 1. Define the Scope  
- **Platforms**: List the social networks, news sites, forums, review sites, and blogs where your brand is likely to appear (e.g., Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, Reddit, Google News, Trustpilot).  
- **Keywords**: Include the exact brand name, common misspellings, product names, hashtags, and any relevant slogans.  

### 2. Choose Monitoring Tools  
| Tool | Core Strength | Typical Use‑Case |
|------|---------------|------------------|
| **Brandwatch / Talkwalker** | Powerful AI‑driven sentiment analysis across thousands of sources | Comprehensive, enterprise‑level monitoring |
| **Mention** | Real‑time alerts, easy setup, integrates with Slack/Email | Small‑to‑mid‑size teams needing quick notifications |
| **Google Alerts** | Free, covers web pages and news | Baseline coverage for low‑traffic mentions |
| **Hootsuite / Sprout Social** | Social‑media specific listening, scheduling, reporting | Ongoing social‑media engagement and response |

Select one primary platform for daily alerts and a secondary, broader tool for periodic deep‑dive reports.  

### 3. Set Up Alerts & Dashboards  
1. **Create keyword streams** in the chosen tool, applying Boolean operators to capture variations (e.g., `"Acme Corp" OR AcmeCorp OR #Acme`).  
2. **Configure sentiment filters** to separate positive, neutral, and negative mentions.  
3. **Schedule automated reports** (daily summary for rapid response, weekly trend analysis for strategy).  

### 4. Process & Respond  
- **Triage**: Assign a severity level (e.g., high for crises, medium for complaints, low for neutral chatter).  
- **Response Playbooks**: Draft template replies for common scenarios (customer service issues, misinformation, praise).  
- **Escalation**: Route high‑severity items to PR or legal as defined in your internal SOP.  

### 5. Analyze & Optimize  
- **KPIs**: Track volume of mentions, sentiment shift, share of voice versus competitors, and response time.  
- **Insights**: Identify emerging topics, influencer mentions, and gaps in brand perception.  
- **Iterate**: Refine keyword lists and alert thresholds monthly based on performance data.

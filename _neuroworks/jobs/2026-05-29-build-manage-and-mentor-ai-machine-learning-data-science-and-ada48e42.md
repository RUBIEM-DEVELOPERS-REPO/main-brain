### 2. Hiring & Onboarding  

1. **Talent Mapping** – Conduct a gap analysis against strategic AI goals; define required skill sets for each function.  
2. **Diversity Pipeline** – Partner with universities, hackathons, and women‑in‑tech organizations; use blind résumé screening to broaden candidate pool.

   - **60 days:** Small‑scale project ownership under a mentor.

4. **Mentor Pairing** – Assign each junior member a senior mentor for bi‑weekly knowledge‑transfer sessions and career guidance.

| Frequency | Focus | Deliverables |

### 4. Mentorship & Career Development  

- **Individual Development Plans (IDPs):** Co‑create with each team member, aligning personal goals with project needs.  
- **Learning Budget:** Allocate $2,000 per employee annually for courses, conferences, or certifications.  
- **Tech Talks & Brown‑Bag Sessions:** Monthly internal seminars where senior staff present recent research or case studies.  
- **Cross‑Functional Rotations:** Offer 2‑month stints in adjacent functions (e.g., data engineering ↔ model development) to broaden expertise.

- **Collaboration:** GitHub Enterprise, Confluence, and Slack integrations for code review and knowledge sharing.  

### 6. Metrics & KPIs

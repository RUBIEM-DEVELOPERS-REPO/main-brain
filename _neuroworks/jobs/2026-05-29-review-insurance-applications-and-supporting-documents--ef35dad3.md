## Job Summary – Insurance Application Reviewer  

The Insurance Application Reviewer is responsible for evaluating incoming insurance applications and all accompanying documentation to determine eligibility and compliance with company policies and regulatory standards.

| Core Activity | What It Involves |
|---|---|
| **Application Intake** | Receive and log each application, confirming that all required fields are completed. |
| **Document Verification** | Examine supporting documents (identification, medical records, financial statements) for authenticity, completeness, and relevance. |
| **Eligibility Assessment** | Apply underwriting guidelines and policy rules to decide whether the applicant meets coverage criteria. |
| **Risk Evaluation** | Identify red‑flags or inconsistencies that may affect risk rating or require further investigation. |
| **Decision Communication** | Draft clear approval, denial, or request‑for‑more‑information notices to be sent to applicants or agents. |
| **Record Keeping** | Update the internal tracking system with decisions, notes, and any follow‑up actions required. |
| **Compliance & Auditing** | Ensure reviews adhere to legal, regulatory, and internal audit standards; flag potential compliance issues. |
| **Collaboration** | Work with underwriters, claims specialists, and customer‑service teams to resolve complex cases. |
| **Continuous Improvement** | Provide feedback on process enhancements and documentation practices. |

**Key Responsibilities**  
- Log and verify each application’s completeness.  
- Authenticate supporting documents and assess relevance.  
- Apply underwriting guidelines to evaluate eligibility.  
- Highlight risk factors and inconsistencies for further review.  
- Communicate decisions promptly to applicants or agents.  
- Maintain accurate records in the tracking system.  
- Uphold compliance with legal and regulatory requirements.  
- Coordinate with underwriting and claims teams on complex cases.  
- Contribute to ongoing process improvements.  

**Performance Indicators**  
- Percentage of applications processed within target turnaround time.  
- Accuracy rate of eligibility determinations.  
- Number of compliance issues identified and resolved.  
- Stakeholder satisfaction scores for communication clarity.

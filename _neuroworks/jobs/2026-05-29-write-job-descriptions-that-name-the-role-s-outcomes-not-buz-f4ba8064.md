## Outcome‑Focused Job Description Template

| Section | Purpose | Example Content |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| **Job Title** | Clear, specific role name | *Senior Data Analyst – Customer Insights* |
| **Primary Outcome** | One sentence stating the main deliverable | *Drive actionable insights that increase customer retention by 5% within the first year.* |
| **Key Responsibilities** | List concrete tasks that enable the outcome | *Collect and analyze churn data.<br>Build predictive models to identify at‑risk segments.<br>Present findings to product and marketing teams.* |
| **Success Metrics** | Quantifiable measures of performance | *Retention lift ≥5% YoY.<br>Model accuracy >90%.<br>Stakeholder satisfaction score ≥8/10.* |
| **Required Skills** | Specific competencies tied to responsibilities | *SQL, Python, statistical modeling.<br>Experience with cohort analysis.* |
| **Preferred Experience** | Relevant background that supports outcome | *3+ years in customer analytics at a SaaS company.* |
| **Collaboration** | Who the role works with | *Product, Marketing, Customer Success.* |
| **Reporting Line** | Who the role reports to | *Director of Analytics.* |

### How to Use This Template

1. **Define the Outcome First** – Start with a single, measurable goal that the role is expected to achieve.  
2. **Translate the Outcome into Actions** – Break the outcome into specific responsibilities that directly contribute to it.  
3. **Attach Metrics** – For each responsibility, list the metric that will prove success.  
4. **Keep Language Action‑Oriented** – Avoid buzzwords like “team player” or “dynamic”; use verbs that describe concrete work (e.g., *build*, *analyze*, *present*).  
5. **Validate with Stakeholders** – Confirm that the outcome aligns with business objectives and that the metrics are realistic.

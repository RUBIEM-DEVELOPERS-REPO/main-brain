## Event Communications Coordination Plan – Tech Forum  

**Objective**  
Synchronize all marketing touch‑points for the upcoming tech forum to maximize attendance, brand visibility, and post‑event engagement.  

**Key Communication Channels**  

| Channel | Timing | Content Focus | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| Email newsletters | 6 weeks → 1 day before event | Save‑the‑date, speaker highlights, agenda preview, registration CTA | Email Marketing Lead |
| Social media (LinkedIn, Twitter, X, Reddit) | 5 weeks → live‑day | Countdown graphics, speaker quotes, behind‑the‑scenes clips, live‑stream reminders | Social Media Manager |
| Press release | 4 weeks before | Official announcement, partner logos, key statistics, media contact | PR Specialist |
| Blog post / website banner | 3 weeks before | In‑depth article on forum theme, sponsor spotlights, registration link | Content Writer |
| Paid ads (LinkedIn, Google) | 2 weeks → event day | Targeted ad copy, registration landing page, retargeting | Paid Media Manager |
| On‑site signage & digital screens | Event day | QR codes for live Q&A, sponsor acknowledgments, schedule overview | Event Ops Lead |
| Post‑event follow‑up | 1 day → 2 weeks after | Thank‑you email, recorded sessions, survey link, next‑event teaser | Email Marketing Lead |

**Workflow Overview**  

1. **Kick‑off (Week -6)** – Align on theme, speakers, and key dates; assign owners.  
2. **Content Calendar** – Draft a master schedule in the shared project board; lock copy approvals 48 hours before each release.  
3. **Creative Assets** – Design team produces templates (email, social graphics, banner) by Week -4; assets stored in the central asset library.  
4. **Approval Loop** – Legal and compliance review all external communications within 24 hours of submission.  
5. **Distribution** – Automated tools (Mailchimp, Hootsuite, LinkedIn Campaign Manager) handle scheduled sends; manual checks 1 hour before launch.  
6. **Monitoring** – Real‑time dashboards track registration clicks, social engagement, and media pickups; adjust spend or messaging as needed.  
7. **Post‑Event Reporting** – Compile metrics (open rates, CTR, attendee count, sentiment) into a concise performance report for stakeholders.  

**Risk Mitigation**  

- **Speaker changes** – Maintain a “last‑minute update” protocol: pre‑approved placeholder copy and rapid‑approval channel.  
- **Platform outages** – Duplicate registration links across email and landing page; have a backup URL ready.  
- **Compliance** – Ensure all GDPR/CCPA consent statements are present in email footers and registration forms.  

**Next Steps**  

- Confirm speaker list and final agenda by **[date]**.  
- Approve budget for paid media spend.  
- Set up shared folder structure for assets and copy drafts.  

*Assumed: The tech forum is a single‑day, virtual‑plus‑in‑person hybrid event; the plan focuses on a 6‑week lead‑time typical for mid‑size industry gatherings.*

### Core Responsibilities  

| Activity | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Coordinate with agents and brokers** | Respond to inquiries, request missing documents, and obtain clarifications on risk details or coverage needs. |

| **Facilitate communication** | Act as the point‑of‑contact among underwriting, claims, and the external sales force, keeping all parties informed of status and next steps. |

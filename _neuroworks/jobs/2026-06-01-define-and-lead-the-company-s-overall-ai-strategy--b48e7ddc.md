| 4 | **Customer Experience** | Net Promoter Score (NPS) improvement | +15 points |

| 6 | **Responsible AI** | Compliance audit score | 100 % compliance |
| 7 | **Innovation Pipeline** | Number of AI prototypes to production | 12 per year |

| Layer | Responsibility | Key Processes | Oversight |
|-------|----------------|---------------|-----------|

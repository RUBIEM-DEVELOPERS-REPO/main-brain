## Monitoring Brand Mentions – A Quick‑Start Guide for Marketing Specialists  

### Overview  
Effective brand monitoring captures real‑time conversations about your company across social media, news outlets, forums, review sites, and emerging platforms. Continuous tracking enables rapid response to opportunities, crisis mitigation, and data‑driven insights for campaign optimisation.  

### Core Platforms to Track  

| Platform | Why It Matters | Typical Tools |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Social Networks** (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook) | High‑volume, public sentiment; viral potential | Brandwatch, Sprout Social, Hootsuite |
| **News & Blogs** | Authority signals; SEO impact | Google News alerts, Meltwater, Mention |
| **Review Sites** (Google Reviews, Trustpilot, G2, Capterra) | Direct customer feedback; purchase influence | ReviewTrackers, Yext |
| **Forums & Communities** (Reddit, Quora, industry‑specific boards) | In‑depth discussions; early trend spotting | Talkwalker, custom RSS feeds |
| **Emerging Channels** (Discord, Clubhouse, niche apps) | Early adopters; niche audience insights | Manual keyword searches, platform‑specific monitoring APIs |

### Process Flow  

1. **Define Keywords** – Include brand name, product names, common misspellings, and relevant hashtags.  
2. **Set Up Alerts** – Configure real‑time notifications for spikes or negative sentiment.  
3. **Aggregate Data** – Use a central dashboard to normalise metrics (volume, reach, sentiment).  
4. **Analyze** – Identify trends, sentiment shifts, and key influencers.  
5. **Act** – Route actionable items to PR, support, or product teams; schedule responses or content amplification.  

### Recommended Tool Stack  

- **Listening Hub:** Brandwatch or Talkwalker for cross‑platform aggregation.  
- **Social Scheduler:** Sprout Social for response coordination.  
- **Sentiment Engine:** MonkeyLearn or native AI modules for automated sentiment scoring.  
- **Reporting:** Google Data Studio or Power BI to visualise KPI trends (mention volume, share of voice, sentiment over time).  

### Best Practices  

- **Regularly Refresh Keywords** to capture new product releases or campaign tags.  
- **Prioritise High‑Impact Sources** (e.g., verified news outlets, high‑follower accounts).  
- **Establish Response Protocols** with clear escalation paths for negative spikes.  
- **Document Findings** in a weekly digest for stakeholders, highlighting actionable insights and emerging opportunities.

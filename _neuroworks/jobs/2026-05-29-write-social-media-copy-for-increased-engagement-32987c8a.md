## Social‑Media Copy Recommendations for Higher Engagement  

**Objective** – Generate more likes, comments, and shares while reinforcing brand personality and driving traffic to the landing page.  

**Key Tactics**  

1. **Hook‑First Headlines** – Begin each post with a bold question, surprising fact, or emoji that stops the scroll.  
2. **Storytelling Micro‑Narratives** – Use a three‑sentence arc (setup → conflict → resolution) to make the message relatable and memorable.  
3. **Clear Call‑to‑Action (CTA)** – Pair every post with a single, specific action (e.g., “Tap to claim your free guide”).  
4. **User‑Generated Content (UGC) Prompts** – Invite followers to share their experiences using a branded hashtag; repost the best entries.  
5. **Platform‑Specific Formatting** –  
   - **Twitter/X:** ≤ 280 characters, include 1‑2 hashtags and a link.  
   - **Instagram:** Use a short caption (1‑2 lines) followed by a carousel or Reel; add up to 10 hashtags at the bottom.  
   - **LinkedIn:** Longer, value‑driven copy (3‑4 sentences) with a professional tone and a single industry hashtag.  

**Sample Copy**  

| Platform | Copy (≈150 char) |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Twitter/X** | 🚀 Ready to double your productivity? Discover the 3‑step workflow that top marketers swear by. 👉 [link] #MarketingTips #Productivity |
| **Instagram** | 🎯 Goal: 10× more leads. <br>Step 1: Capture attention with bold visuals. <br>Step 2: Offer a free audit. <br>Step 3: Follow up fast. <br>#LeadGen #GrowthHack |
| **LinkedIn** | Marketers, are you still using outdated funnels? Learn the modern 5‑stage model that boosts conversion rates by 27 %—download the free guide today. #B2BMarketing |

**Implementation Checklist**  

- Draft a content calendar aligning copy with upcoming campaigns.  
- Pre‑design visual assets that complement each headline.  
- Set up tracking URLs (UTM) for every CTA.  
- Schedule A/B tests on headline phrasing and CTA wording.  

*Assumed: the brand already has visual assets and a landing page; the copy is intended for a B2B SaaS marketing audience.*

## Coordinating Event Communications for Tech Forums  

### 1. Define Communication Objectives  
- Clarify the purpose of each forum (brand awareness, lead generation, product launch).  
- Establish key performance indicators (registration numbers, attendee engagement, post‑event sentiment).  

### 2. Build a Timeline  
| Phase | Milestones | Typical Lead Time |
|-------|------------|-------------------|
| Planning | Audience profiling, speaker lineup, venue/virtual platform selection | 8–12 weeks |
| Pre‑event | Save‑the‑date, teaser content, registration launch, media outreach | 6–8 weeks |
| Ramp‑up | Reminder emails, social media countdown, press releases, partner promotions | 2–4 weeks |
| Live | Real‑time updates, live‑stream links, on‑site signage, moderator briefs | Event day |
| Post‑event | Thank‑you notes, recordings, surveys, recap blog, social highlights | 1–2 weeks |

### 3. Audience Segmentation & Messaging  
- **Developers & Engineers:** Emphasize technical deep‑dives, code labs, and speaker expertise.  
- **Business Leaders:** Highlight strategic insights, ROI case studies, and networking opportunities.  
- **Media & Influencers:** Provide exclusive interview slots, press kits, and high‑resolution assets.  

### 4. Channel Mix  
- **Email:** Structured drip campaigns (announcement, reminder, last‑call, follow‑up).  
- **Social Media:** Platform‑specific teasers (LinkedIn for professionals, Twitter/X for real‑time updates, YouTube for teaser videos).  
- **Website/Landing Page:** Central hub with agenda, speaker bios, registration form, and FAQ.  
- **Paid Media:** Targeted LinkedIn Sponsored Content and tech‑site banner ads for broader reach.  
- **Partner Networks:** Co‑branded announcements through industry associations and sponsors.  

### 5. Content Production  
- Create a content calendar aligning each channel with the timeline.  
- Develop reusable assets: speaker headshots, session abstracts, banner graphics, short promo videos.  
- Draft press releases and media advisories following the organization’s style guide.  

### 6. Stakeholder Coordination  
- Hold a kickoff call with speakers, sponsors, and internal teams to confirm deliverables.  
- Assign a point of contact for each channel (email copy, social media, PR).  
- Use a shared project board (e.g., Trello or Asana) to track tasks, deadlines, and approvals.  

### 7. Measurement & Optimization  
- Track open/click rates for emails, engagement metrics for social posts, and registration conversion rates.  
- Conduct a post‑event debrief to assess KPI performance and capture lessons learned for future forums.

## Overview  

Effective digital‑marketing campaign management combines strategic planning, data‑driven execution, and continuous optimisation. The following framework helps a Marketing Specialist run campaigns that achieve measurable results while staying agile.

## 1. Define Clear Objectives  

- Align campaign goals with overall business targets (e.g., lead generation, e‑commerce sales, brand awareness).  
- Use SMART criteria (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time‑bound).  

## 2. Audience Segmentation  

- Build detailed buyer personas based on demographics, psychographics, and behavioural data.  
- Leverage first‑party data (website analytics, CRM) and third‑party insights to create high‑precision segments.  

## 3. Channel Selection & Budget Allocation  

| Channel | Typical Use‑Case | KPI Examples |
|---------|------------------|--------------|
| Search (SEM) | Intent‑driven acquisition | Cost‑per‑Click, Conversion Rate |
| Social (Meta, LinkedIn, TikTok) | Awareness & engagement | CPM, Click‑Through Rate, Engagement Rate |
| Display & Programmatic | Retargeting & prospecting | View‑through Conversions |
| Email | Nurture & retention | Open Rate, Revenue per Email |

- Allocate budget proportionally to channel performance forecasts and test budgets for emerging platforms.  

## 4. Creative & Messaging Strategy  

- Develop a consistent brand voice and visual style across assets.  
- Use A/B testing for headlines, images, and calls‑to‑action to identify top‑performing variations.  

## 5. Campaign Execution  

- Set up tracking infrastructure: UTM parameters, conversion pixels, and attribution models (e.g., data‑driven attribution).  
- Schedule launches using a content calendar that coordinates paid, owned, and earned media.  

## 6. Monitoring & Optimisation  

- Review performance dashboards daily for spend, ROAS, and KPI trends.  
- Apply rule‑based adjustments (e.g., pause under‑performing ads, increase bids on high‑ROI placements).  
- Conduct weekly deep‑dive analyses to refine audience targeting, creative assets, and bidding strategies.  

## 7. Reporting & Insights  

- Produce a concise weekly report highlighting key metrics, insights, and action items.  
- Summarise learnings for future campaigns, documenting what worked, what didn’t, and why.  

## 8. Continuous Learning  

- Stay updated on platform algorithm changes, privacy regulations, and emerging ad formats.  
- Invest in skill development (e.g., certifications in Google Ads, Meta Blueprint).

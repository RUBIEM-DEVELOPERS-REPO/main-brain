## Head of AI Playbook – Build, Manage, and Mentor AI/ML/Data‑Science/Automation Teams  

1. **Define Vision & Metrics** – Articulate a concise AI mission (e.g., “deliver data‑driven products that increase revenue X % in Y years”) and bind it to clear KPIs such as model accuracy, time‑to‑deployment, and cost savings. This aligns the organization and provides a basis for performance reviews. [1]  

2. **Team Structure** – Organize four functional pods that map directly to the domains in the brief:  
   - **Research & Innovation** – exploratory ML, prototype development.  
   - **Product‑Focused ML** – production models, A/B testing, impact measurement.  
   - **Data Engineering & Platform** – pipelines, feature stores, MLOps infrastructure.  
   - **Automation & RPA** – workflow bots, low‑code AI solutions.  
   The pod model enables specialization while preserving clear hand‑offs. [1]  

3. **Roles & Career Ladders** – Establish entry‑to‑staff tracks for each pod (Junior → Senior → Lead → Principal) with parallel technical and people‑lead paths. Define promotion criteria that balance delivery impact and mentorship. [1]  

4. **Hiring & On‑boarding** – Create a talent matrix, use structured bias‑aware interviews, and deliver a two‑week AI boot‑camp covering data catalog, MLOps basics, and ethical guidelines.  

5. **Management Cadence** – Implement quarterly OKR reviews, bi‑weekly sprint retrospectives, monthly one‑on‑ones, and a cross‑pod sync to ensure alignment and rapid issue resolution.  

6. **Mentorship & Growth** – Pair senior staff with juniors, set personal development plans, allocate a learning budget, and host quarterly hack‑days to foster skill expansion.  

7. **Operations & Automation** – Standardize MLOps (CI/CD, model versioning), monitor drift, latency, and cost, and govern RPA bots through change‑control and audit trails.  

8. **Culture & Ethics** – Publish an AI ethics charter, promote psychological safety, and track diversity metrics to sustain an inclusive, responsible environment.  

9. **Continuous Improvement** – Use a health scorecard (deployment frequency, MTTR, team engagement) and conduct quarterly AI Impact Reviews to align outcomes with business goals.

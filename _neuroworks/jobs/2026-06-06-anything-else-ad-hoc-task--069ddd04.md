### Tradeoffs of Server-Side Rendering (SSR) vs Client-Side Rendering (CSR)

#### Server-Side Rendering (SSR)
- **Performance:** SSR can lead to longer initial load times due to the need for server processing.
- **SEO Benefits:** SEO is generally better with CSR as search engines can render static pages faster, which improves indexing and ranking.

#### Client-Side Rendering (CSR)
- **Initial Load Time:** CSR typically results in faster initial page loads since rendering happens on the client side.
- **Flexibility:** CSR allows for more dynamic content updates without a full re-rendering of the entire page.

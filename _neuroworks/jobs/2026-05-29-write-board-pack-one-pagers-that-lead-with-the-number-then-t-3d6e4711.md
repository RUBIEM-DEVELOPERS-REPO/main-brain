## Board‑Pack One‑Pager Template – “Number → Why”

| **Section** | **What to Include** | **Example Text** |
|------------|----------------------|------------------|
| **1️⃣ Key Metric (the number)** | The single figure that best captures the decision’s impact (e.g., revenue growth, cash‑conversion cycle, cost‑to‑serve reduction). | **12 % YoY revenue growth** |
| **2️⃣ Why it matters** | One‑sentence link to strategic objectives, risk, or value creation. Use active verbs such as *drives*, *protects*, *enables*. | **Why?** It validates the new pricing model and lifts EBITDA margin by 150 bps, supporting our 2027 profitability target. |
| **3️⃣ Supporting context (optional)** | • Trend line (last 3‑6 periods)  <br>• Benchmark vs. peers or plan  <br>• Key driver(s) behind the number | • +4 % Q‑on‑Q, outpacing the 2 % industry average. <br>• Driven by an 8 % uplift in subscription renewals. |
| **4️⃣ Action / Decision needed** | Clear, single‑line ask for the board (approve, defer, monitor). | **Approve the incremental marketing spend** to sustain the renewal lift. |
| **5️⃣ Risks / Caveats** | One bullet on the biggest upside or downside that could swing the number. | **Risk:** Churn could rise if price elasticity is higher than modeled. |

### Why This Structure Works
1. **Instant impact** – Executives scan for numbers first; leading with the metric grabs attention and frames the conversation.  
2. **Logical flow** – The “why” follows immediately, turning a raw figure into a strategic insight that drives decision‑making.

## Lead Data Engineer – AI Platform (Remote)

**What success looks like in the first 90 days**  
- Deploy the next‑generation data pipeline that reduces nightly batch latency from 6 hours to under 30 minutes.  
- Deliver a production‑ready feature store that lets data scientists register and serve 10+ ML features with < 5 % error rate.  
- Establish clear monitoring and alerting so the team can spot and resolve data quality issues within 15 minutes of occurrence.  
- Mentor two junior engineers, helping them ship their first pull requests and feel confident navigating our codebase.

### Must‑haves (non‑negotiable)  
1. **5+ years** of hands‑on experience building scalable ETL/ELT pipelines on cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure).  
2. Proven track record with **modern data stack tools** – e.g., Snowflake/BigQuery, dbt, Airflow, Kafka, and Spark.  
3. Strong **SQL and Python** skills; ability to write clean, testable code and automate data quality checks.  
4. Experience designing **feature stores or data products** that serve real‑time ML models.  
5. Ability to communicate technical concepts clearly to both engineers and product teams.

### Compensation & Benefits  
- **Base salary:** $130 k – $165 k USD, depending on experience.  
- **Equity:** up to 0.15 % grant, vested over four years.  
- **Benefits:** health, dental, vision, 401(k) match, unlimited PTO, learning stipend.  

### Remote policy  
- Fully remote role; we welcome candidates in any time zone.  
- Quarterly in‑person meet‑ups (travel reimbursed) to build team chemistry.

### How to apply  
Send a brief cover letter and your résumé to **jobs@ourcompany.com** with the subject line **“Lead Data Engineer – AI Platform”**. Include a short paragraph describing a data pipeline you built that delivered measurable impact. We’ll review applications on a rolling basis and schedule interviews with the hiring manager and the engineering lead.

## Standard Operating Procedure (SOP)  
**Title:** Convert a Business Process into a Formal SOP  

**Owner:** Process Documentation Specialist (PDS)  

### 1. Trigger  
- Receipt of a documented business process that requires formalization into an SOP (e.g., new workflow, regulatory requirement, or internal audit request).  

### 2. Outcome  
- A complete, approved SOP that includes trigger, outcome, prerequisites, numbered steps (with owner, input, action, output, checkpoint, escalation), escalation table for non‑routine cases, linked artifacts, and a change‑log entry.  

### 3. Prerequisites  
| # | Requirement | Owner |
|---|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Source process documentation (flowchart, narrative, or work instructions) | Process Owner |
| 2 | Current version of the SOP template (stored in the SOP repository) | Document Control Lead |
| 3 | Access to the SOP management system (e.g., Confluence, SharePoint) | IT Support |
| 4 | Defined review and approval matrix for the relevant functional area | Governance Manager |

### 4. Procedure – Atomic Steps  

| Step | Owner | Input | Action | Output | Checkpoint | Escalation |
|------|-------|-------|--------|--------|------------|------------|
| 1 | **Process Owner** | Existing process description | Submit the process to the PDS via the SOP Request Form | Request logged in SOP tracker | Request logged with unique ID | If no response within 2 days → **Process Governance Lead** |
| 2 | **Process Documentation Specialist (PDS)** | Request ID, source process | Map the process to SOP template sections; draft trigger, outcome, prerequisites | Draft SOP document (version 0) | Draft completeness verified against template checklist | If missing critical info → **Process Owner** |
| 3 | **Subject Matter Expert (SME)** | Draft SOP | Review for technical accuracy; add/clarify inputs, actions, outputs | Reviewed draft with comments | All SME comments resolved | If disagreement persists → **Process Governance Lead** |
| 4 | **PDS** | Reviewed draft | Incorporate SME feedback; number steps; assign owners; define checkpoints | Revised SOP (version 1) | Internal QA sign‑off (checklist) | If QA fails → **Document Control Lead** |
| 5 | **Document Control Lead** | Revised SOP | Perform formatting audit; attach linked artifacts (e.g., flowcharts, forms) | Final SOP ready for approval | Approval routing completed | If formatting non‑compliant → **PDS** |
| 6 | **Functional Manager** | Final SOP | Approve or reject | Approved SOP (version 1.0) or rejection notes | Approval recorded in SOP system | If rejected → **Process Owner** for rework |
| 7 | **PDS** | Approved SOP | Publish to SOP repository; notify stakeholders; create change‑log entry | SOP live; change log entry created | Publication confirmed | If publishing error → **IT Support** |
| 8 | **Process Owner** | Live SOP | Communicate SOP to affected staff; schedule training if required | Staff acknowledgment record | Training completion verified | If training not completed → **Learning & Development Lead** |

### 5. Escalation Table – Non‑Routine Cases  

| Issue | Primary Escalation Owner | Secondary Escalation Owner | Resolution Timeframe |
|-------|---------------------------|----------------------------|----------------------|
| No response to SOP request | Process Governance Lead | Head of Operations | 2 business days |
| Persistent disagreement on step content | Process Governance Lead | Chief Compliance Officer | 5 business days |
| QA audit failure | Document Control Lead | Quality Assurance Director | 3 business days |
| Publication failure in SOP system | IT Support | Chief Information Officer | 1 business day |
| Training compliance lag | Learning & Development Lead | HR Business Partner | 7 calendar days |

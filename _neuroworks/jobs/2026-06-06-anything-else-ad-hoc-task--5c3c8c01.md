## Answer
Feature Flags offer several tangible benefits to a small team:

1. **Incremental Rollouts:** They allow features to be gradually enabled, enabling teams to test and refine new functionalities before full-scale deployment, reducing the risk of system instability or user dissatisfaction.

2. **Cost-Effective Testing:** Feature flags facilitate controlled experiments without requiring significant changes to production environments, allowing for quick iterations and cost-effective testing of various feature configurations.

3. **Flexibility in Scaling:** They enable teams to turn features on or off based on specific conditions, making it easier to scale operations by selectively enabling features as the team grows or needs change.

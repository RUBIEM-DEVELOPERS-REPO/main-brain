## Campaign Brief  

### Audience  
- **Who:** 25‑45 year‑old urban professionals who regularly shop online for lifestyle products.  
- **Where:** Primarily active on Instagram, TikTok, and Pinterest; also reachable via email newsletters and programmatic display on lifestyle sites.  
- **What they care about:** Sustainable sourcing, convenience, and products that enhance personal style without compromising ethics.  

### Insight (Why now)  
Consumers are increasingly demanding transparency and eco‑friendly options after recent high‑profile supply‑chain scandals; the market is primed for a brand that can prove its sustainability credentials while delivering a seamless digital shopping experience.  

### Hook  
*“Style that feels good—inside and out.”*  

### Channels (ranked with rationale)  
1. **Instagram Reels & Stories** – visual format aligns with style‑focused audience; high engagement rates for short‑form video.  
2. **TikTok** – algorithmic discovery can amplify brand‑first impressions; ideal for trend‑driven sustainability challenges.  
3. **Pinterest Boards** – long‑tail search traffic for “sustainable fashion” drives sustained awareness.  
4. **Programmatic Display (Lifestyle sites)** – retargets users who have engaged with social assets, reinforcing the hook.  
5. **Email Newsletter** – converts warm leads with exclusive offers and behind‑the‑scenes sustainability content.  

### Assets Needed  
- 3‑second teaser video clips (3 variations)  
- 15‑second product showcase reels (2 versions)  
- Brand‑style guide for sustainable messaging  
- High‑resolution lifestyle photography (10 images)  
- Email template with dynamic product blocks  
- Landing page copy and UI mock‑up  

### Success Metric  
- Achieve **3 % click‑through rate (CTR)** on social video ads and **15 % conversion rate** from landing page to purchase within the first 30 days.

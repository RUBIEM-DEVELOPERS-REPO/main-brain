## Answer
Feature Flags offer several key benefits for small teams managing complex applications:

1. **Incremental Rollouts:** They allow features to be gradually introduced, enabling teams to monitor user feedback and performance before fully deploying new functionalities.
2. **Cost-Effective Testing:** Feature Flags enable controlled testing environments where only selected users can access experimental features, reducing the risk of widespread issues and minimizing operational costs associated with full feature rollouts.
3. **Flexibility in Maintenance:** With feature flags, teams can easily turn off or modify active features without affecting other parts of the application, facilitating quick adjustments to maintain stability and security.

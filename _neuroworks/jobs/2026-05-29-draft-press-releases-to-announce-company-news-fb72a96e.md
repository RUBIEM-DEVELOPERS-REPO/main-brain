## Press‑Release Drafts for Company News  

All drafts follow the standard news‑wire structure – headline, sub‑headline, dateline, lead paragraph, body, boiler‑plate, and media contact – and embed the “4 Ps” of marketing (product, price, place, promotion) to deliver clear, compelling messaging.  

### 1. New Product Launch  

**Headline:** *[Company] Unveils [Product Name], a Game‑Changing Solution for [Target Industry]*  

**Sub‑headline:** *Innovative features and competitive pricing set a new benchmark for [product category].*  

**Dateline:** *City, State – Month Day, Year*  

**Lead paragraph:**  
[Company] announced today the launch of **[Product Name]**, a [brief description – e.g., “cloud‑based analytics platform”] designed to help [primary audience] solve [core problem]. The solution combines **[key benefit #1]**, **[key benefit #2]**, and **[key benefit #3]** at a price point that outperforms existing alternatives.  

**Body (2‑3 paragraphs):**  
- **Product details** – highlight unique features, underlying technology, and how it differs from competitors (the “product” P).  
- **Pricing strategy** – describe the pricing model, value proposition, and any introductory offers (the “price” P).  
- **Distribution/availability** – specify where and how customers can purchase or access the product (the “place” P).  
- **Promotion** – include a quote from the VP of Marketing on the launch campaign, media outreach, and upcoming webinars (the “promotion” P).  

**Boiler‑plate:**  
*About [Company]* – a concise, AMA‑style description of the organization, its mission, and core markets.  

**Media contact:**  
Name, Title, Phone, Email

### 2. Executive Appointment  

**Headline:** *[Company] Appoints [Executive Name] as New [Title] to Accelerate [Strategic Initiative]*  

**Sub‑headline:** *Seasoned leader brings 20 years of experience in [relevant field] to drive growth.*  

**Dateline:** *City, State – Month Day, Year*  

**Lead paragraph:**  
[Company] today announced the appointment of **[Executive Name]** as its new **[Title]**. In this role, [Executive] will oversee [key responsibility] and spearhead [strategic initiative], positioning the company for accelerated growth in [market/segment].  

**Body (2‑3 paragraphs):**  
- **Background** – summarize the executive’s prior experience, notable achievements, and relevance to the new role.  
- **Strategic impact** – explain how the appointment aligns with the company’s long‑term objectives and upcoming projects.  
- **Quote** – include statements from the CEO and the newly appointed executive on expectations and vision.  

**Boiler‑plate:**  
*About [Company]* – brief corporate overview.  

**Media contact:**  
Name, Title, Phone, Email

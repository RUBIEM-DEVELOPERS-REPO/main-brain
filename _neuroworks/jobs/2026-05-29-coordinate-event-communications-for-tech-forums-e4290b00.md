## Event Communications Coordination Plan  
**Role:** Marketing Specialist – Tech Forum Series  

### Objectives  
- Raise awareness and drive attendance for each forum.  
- Ensure consistent branding and messaging across all channels.  
- Foster engagement before, during, and after the events.

### Key Channels & Tactics  
- **Email Campaigns** – three‑step sequence (announcement, reminder, post‑event recap).  
- **Social Media** – scheduled posts on LinkedIn, Twitter, and relevant Discord/Slack communities; use event hashtags and speaker tags.  
- **Press & Partnerships** – distribute press releases to industry newsletters; coordinate co‑marketing with partner organizations.  
- **Website & Landing Pages** – dedicated event pages with registration forms, speaker bios, and agenda PDFs.  
- **On‑site Messaging** – digital signage, live‑stream links, and real‑time social‑media walls.

### Timeline (8‑Week Cycle)  
| Week | Activity |
|------|----------|
| 1 | Finalize event theme, speakers, and agenda; create branding assets. |
| 2 | Publish landing page; send “Save the Date” email; announce on social media. |
| 3 | Release press kit; begin partner outreach. |
| 4 | First reminder email; launch targeted LinkedIn ads. |
| 5 | Publish speaker spotlight posts; engage community Q&A sessions. |
| 6 | Second reminder email with early‑bird discount; retarget ads. |
| 7 | Final reminder email (24 h before); schedule live‑stream links. |
| 8 | Post‑event thank‑you email; share recordings and highlights; collect feedback. |

### Content Checklist  
- Event title, date, time, and time‑zone.  
- Clear value proposition (“Learn the latest AI scaling techniques”).  
- Speaker headshots and short bios.  
- Registration CTA with tracking UTM parameters.  
- Accessibility notes (closed captions, sign‑language support).  

### Measurement & Reporting  
- **KPIs:** registration conversion rate, email open/click rates, social‑media impressions, live‑stream attendance, post‑event survey score.  
- **Tools:** Mailchimp (email), Hootsuite (social scheduling), Google Analytics (landing page), Zoom/YouTube analytics (stream).  
- Generate a post‑event performance report within 5 days of the forum.  

*Assumed: The tech forums are a recurring series requiring a repeatable communications workflow; details such as exact dates and speaker list will be supplied by the event lead.*

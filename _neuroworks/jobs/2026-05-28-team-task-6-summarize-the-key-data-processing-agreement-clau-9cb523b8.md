# Key GDPR‑Required Data‑Processing‑Agreement Clauses

## 1. Subject‑Matter and Duration of Processing  
**What it covers:** The specific personal data to be processed, the purpose(s) of the processing, and the time period over which the processor will act.  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “subject‑matter and duration of the processing”【1】.

## 2. Nature and Purpose of Processing  
**What it covers:** Detailed description of the activities the processor will perform on the data.  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “nature and purpose of the processing”【1】.

## 3. Type of Personal Data & Categories of Data Subjects  
**What it covers:** Identification of the data types (e.g., contact info, health data) and the categories of individuals affected.  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “type of personal data and categories of data subjects”【1】.

## 4. Controller’s Obligations & Rights  
**What it covers:** The controller’s instructions, responsibilities for compliance, and rights to audit or supervise the processor.  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “obligations and rights of the controller”【1】.

## 5. Processor‑Only on Written Instructions  
**What it covers:** The processor may act solely on documented instructions from the controller and cannot process data for its own purposes.  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “processor shall process personal data only on documented instructions”【1】.

## 6. Confidentiality  
**What it covers:** All individuals authorized to process the data must be bound by confidentiality obligations.  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “persons authorised to process the personal data shall be under an appropriate confidentiality obligation”【1】.

## 7. Technical & Organisational Security Measures  
**What it covers:** Measures that satisfy the standard of Article 32 (security of processing).  
**GDPR reference:** Article 28 § 3(1) – “appropriate technical and organisational measures”【1】.

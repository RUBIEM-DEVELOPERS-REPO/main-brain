## Event Communications Coordination Plan  
**Role:** Marketing Specialist – Tech Forum Coordination  

### 1. Objectives  
- Raise awareness of each forum among target developers, engineers, and industry analysts.  
- Drive registration and attendance to meet or exceed the projected 75 % capacity fill‑rate.  
- Foster post‑event engagement to nurture leads for future initiatives.

### 2. Audience Segmentation  
| Segment | Channels | Messaging Focus |
|---|---|---|
| **Developers & Engineers** | Developer newsletters, GitHub Discussions, Reddit (r/programming, r/technology) | Technical agenda, speaker expertise, hands‑on workshops |
| **Product Managers & Executives** | LinkedIn Sponsored Content, industry newsletters, direct email outreach | Business impact, ROI, networking opportunities |
| **Media & Analysts** | Press release distribution (PR Newswire), targeted pitches, Twitter Spaces | Thought‑leadership angles, exclusive interview offers |

### 3. Communication Timeline  
| Milestone | Lead‑time | Deliverable |
|---|---|---|
| **Save‑the‑Date Announcement** | 8 weeks before | Branded graphic + brief copy for email & social |
| **Full Agenda Release** | 6 weeks before | Detailed agenda PDF, speaker bios, speaker‑tagged social posts |
| **Early‑Bird Registration Push** | 5 weeks before | Email blast with discount code, LinkedIn carousel |
| **Reminder Series** | 3 weeks, 1 week, 2 days before | Countdown graphics, video teaser, FAQ sheet |
| **Live‑Day Updates** | Event day | Real‑time tweets, Instagram Stories, Slack channel updates |
| **Post‑Event Follow‑Up** | 2 days after | Thank‑you email, recording links, survey, next‑event teaser |

### 4. Content Assets  
- **Visuals:** Consistent color palette, logo lock‑up, speaker headshots.  
- **Copy Templates:** Email subject lines, social captions, press release boilerplate.  
- **Multimedia:** Short speaker intro videos (30 s), animated agenda teaser (15 s).  

### 5. Measurement & Optimization  
- **KPIs:** Registrations, open/click‑through rates, social engagement (likes, retweets, shares), post‑event survey satisfaction.  
- **Tools:** HubSpot for email automation, Hootsuite for social scheduling, Google Analytics for landing‑page tracking.  
- **Review Cycle:** Weekly performance check; adjust messaging or spend allocation based on real‑time data.

### 6. Stakeholder Collaboration  
- **Product Team:** Provide technical details for agenda items.  
- **Design Team:** Deliver assets 2 weeks ahead of each milestone.  
- **Sales Ops:** Align lead‑capture forms with CRM fields.  

*Assumed: The tech forum series runs quarterly and targets a mixed audience of developers, product leaders, and media.*

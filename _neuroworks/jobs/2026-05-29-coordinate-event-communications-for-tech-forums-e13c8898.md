## Coordinating Event Communications for Tech Forums  

### 1. Define Communication Objectives  
- Clarify the purpose of each forum (e.g., brand awareness, lead generation, community building).  
- Set measurable goals such as registration numbers, attendee engagement rates, and post‑event content reach.  

### 2. Build a Timeline  
| Phase | Key Activities | Typical Lead Time |
|-------|----------------|-------------------|
| Pre‑launch | Announce event, open registration, teaser content | 6–8 weeks before |
| Mid‑campaign | Speaker spotlights, agenda releases, reminder emails | 4–2 weeks before |
| Final push | Last‑call registration, logistics details, social‑media countdown | 1 week before |
| Post‑event | Thank‑you notes, recordings, surveys, highlight reels | 1–3 days after |

### 3. Audience Segmentation  
- **Prospects:** Emphasize value propositions and early‑bird incentives.  
- **Existing customers:** Highlight advanced sessions, networking opportunities, and product demos.  
- **Partners & sponsors:** Provide co‑branding assets and speaking slots.  

### 4. Channel Mix  
- **Email:** Structured series (announcement, reminder, final details).  
- **Social media:** Platform‑specific posts (LinkedIn for professional audience, Twitter for real‑time updates).  
- **Webpage/Landing page:** Central hub with registration form, agenda, and speaker bios.  
- **Paid ads:** Targeted LinkedIn and programmatic display ads for broader reach.  

### 5. Content Guidelines  
- Keep messaging concise (≤ 150 characters for social posts).  
- Use consistent branding (logo, color palette, tagline).  
- Include clear calls‑to‑action (e.g., “Register now”, “Add to calendar”).  
- Provide accessibility information (captions, live‑stream options).  

### 6. Coordination & Approval Workflow  
1. Draft copy → 2. Review by product & legal → 3. Final sign‑off by Marketing Lead → 4. Schedule in automation platform.  

### 7. Measurement & Optimization  
- Track open/click rates, registration conversions, and social engagement.  
- Conduct A/B tests on subject lines and creative assets.  
- Compile a post‑mortem report with insights for the next forum.

## Event Communications Coordination Plan – Tech Forum  

**Objective**  
Ensure consistent, timely, and engaging communication across all channels to maximize attendance, sponsor visibility, and participant satisfaction for the upcoming tech forum.

### 1. Communication Timeline  
| Phase | Key Activities | Channels | Owner |
|-------|----------------|----------|-------|
| **Pre‑Launch (8‑12 weeks out)** | Announce event theme, keynote speakers, and registration opening. | Email newsletter, website banner, LinkedIn, Twitter, industry partner newsletters. | Marketing Specialist |
| **Early Promotion (6‑8 weeks)** | Release speaker bios, agenda highlights, early‑bird ticket reminders. | Blog post, LinkedIn articles, Twitter threads, targeted ads, community forums. | Content Writer / Paid Media |
| **Mid‑Campaign (3‑5 weeks)** | Publish sponsor spotlights, session deep‑dives, countdown graphics. | Email drip, Instagram Stories/Reels, YouTube teaser, Slack/Discord community posts. | Graphic Designer / Social Media Lead |
| **Final Push (1‑2 weeks)** | Last‑call registration, logistical details (venue, virtual platform links), FAQ distribution. | Email blast, SMS reminder (opt‑in), pinned forum posts, live Q&A on LinkedIn Live. | Marketing Specialist |
| **During Event** | Real‑time updates, session reminders, live‑tweeting, photo/video sharing. | Event app push notifications, Twitter, Instagram, LinkedIn, live stream chat. | Social Media Coordinator |
| **Post‑Event (1‑2 weeks)** | Thank‑you notes, recordings, survey distribution, highlight reel. | Email, website “Resources” page, LinkedIn article, partner newsletters. | Marketing Specialist |

### 2. Messaging Framework  
- **Brand Voice:** Professional yet approachable; emphasize innovation and community.  
- **Core Themes:** Thought leadership, networking opportunities, cutting‑edge tech demos.  
- **Call‑to‑Action:** Register now, submit questions, share on social, download resources.

### 3. Content Assets  
- Event logo and visual kit (color palette, typography).  
- Pre‑written email templates (announcement, reminder, last‑call).  
- Social media graphics (hero images, speaker cards, countdown timers).  
- FAQ document and speaker one‑pagers for media outreach.

### 4. Stakeholder Collaboration  
- **Speakers:** Provide bios, headshots, and session abstracts 4 weeks before launch.  
- **Sponsors:** Align branding placements in emails and on‑site signage; obtain approved logos.  
- **Product Teams:** Supply demo videos and technical assets for promotional clips.  
- **Community Managers:** Coordinate cross‑posting in relevant forums and Discord channels.

### 5. Measurement & Optimization  
- Track open/click rates, registration conversion, social engagement (impressions, retweets, shares).  
- Conduct A/B testing on subject lines and post timing.  
- Post‑event survey to gauge communication effectiveness and gather improvement ideas.

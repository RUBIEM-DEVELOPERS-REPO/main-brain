1. **No Champion** – Absence of an internal advocate who actively pushes the opportunity forward.  
2. **No Compelling Event** – Lack of a time‑bound business driver or market pressure that creates urgency for purchase.  
3. **Ghost Buyer** – The primary contact is not the actual decision‑maker, risking mis‑aligned communication and stalled progress.

**Escalation Actions**  
- **Flag in CRM:** Mark the opportunity with the appropriate risk tag (e.g., “No Champion”).

- **Validate Buyer Authority:** Conduct a quick qualification call or request an organizational chart to confirm the decision‑maker’s identity; bring the verified buyer into the next discussion.

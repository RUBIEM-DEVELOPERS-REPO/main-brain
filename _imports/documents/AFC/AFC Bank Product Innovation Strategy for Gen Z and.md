# AFC Bank Product Innovation Strategy for Gen Z and Pensioners

## Introduction

This document outlines a unified product strategy for AFC Bank targeting __Gen Z \(18–35\)__ and __Pensioners__ in Zimbabwe\. The goal is to design banking services that are easy to implement, address clear customer pain points, and generate sustainable revenue for the bank\.

The products focus on __transaction banking, ecosystem partnerships, and service differentiation__, rather than lending products, making them easier to deploy and scale\.

# SECTION 1: GEN Z PRODUCTS

## 1\. AFC Young Hustler Account

### Description

A low‑fee digital transaction account designed for genz informal traders, freelancers, and gig workers operating in Zimbabwe’s informal economy within the age threshhold of __\(18–35\)\.__

### Unique Selling Point

Unlike traditional youth accounts, the Hustler Account is specifically designed as a __business transaction account for young informal entrepreneurs__, allowing them to manage business payments and collections easily\.

### How the Product Works

Customers open the account digitally using a national ID and mobile number\. The account supports:

Customer payments n\- Supplier payments

QR merchant payments

Wallet transfers

The account acts as a __digital wallet for small businesses__\.

### Customer Benefits

Easy digital account opening

Low transaction fees

Ability to manage small business payments

Safer alternative to cash

### Bank Benefits

Rapid customer acquisition among youth

Increased digital transactions

Merchant payment ecosystem growth

### Implementation Steps

Configure Hustler Account in core banking system\.

Enable digital onboarding through mobile banking and USSD\.

Integrate wallet rails \(ZIPIT, EcoCash, OneMoney, innbucks\)\.

Launch merchant QR payment acceptance\.

Introduce debit card issuance for Hustler accounts\.

### Financial Model

Revenue Streams:

Transaction fees

Merchant QR fees

Debit card interchange

Projection:

Users: 50,000 Average transactions: 20/month Average per user fee: $0\.10

Annual revenue potential:

$1\.2M

## 2\. AFC AgriStarter Kit \(service\)\*

### Description

A program designed to help young people enter agriculture through access to suppliers, advisory support, and agricultural marketplaces\.

### Unique Selling Point

Instead of focusing only on agricultural loans like competitors, AFC becomes an __agriculture ecosystem platform linking farmers, suppliers and buyers__\. Young start up farmers\. 

### How the Product Works

The program connects farmers to:

Seed suppliers

Fertilizer suppliers

Agricultural advisory services

Commodity buyers

### Customer Benefits

Easier entry into farming

Reliable access to inputs

Access to agricultural knowledge

### Bank Benefits

Positions AFC as the agriculture bank

Increased agricultural transactions

Supplier partnership revenue

### Implementation Steps

Build supplier partnerships with seed and fertilizer companies\.

Integrate supplier payments through AFC accounts\.

Launch advisory SMS farming alerts\.  Partner with agricultural cooperatives\.

### Financial Model

Revenue Streams:

Supplier commissions

Agricultural marketplace payments

Example:

10,000 farmers Average $500 seasonal input purchase Commission 1%

Annual revenue potential:

$50,000–$150,000 initially

AFC Bank Product Innovation Strategy for Gen Z
and Pensioners
Introduction
This document outlines proposed banking products for AFC Bank (formerly Agribank) based on an analysis
of Zimbabwean financial institutions and the gaps in products and services offered to two key demographic
groups: Gen Z (18–35) and Pensioners.
The goal is to design high-impact, easy-to-implement products that leverage AFC Bank's agricultural
mandate and development banking strengths.
SECTION 1: GEN Z PRODUCT OPPORTUNITIES
1. AFC Hustler Account
Description: A zero-fee digital account designed for freelancers, informal traders and young entrepreneurs.
Key Features: - No monthly maintenance fees - Mobile wallet integration - Transaction-based microcredit -
Simple bookkeeping dashboard
Why It Works: Zimbabwe's youth economy is largely informal. Traditional banks require collateral or formal
income proof, excluding young entrepreneurs. This account builds credit profiles based on transaction
history rather than formal employment.

Conclusion
AFC Bank has a unique strategic opportunity to position itself as the bank that connects Zimbabwe's youth
and pensioners through agriculture, financial inclusion and development banking.
By focusing on simple but impactful financial solutions, AFC Bank can grow its customer base while
supporting national economic development.

__4\.  Value\-Added Services & Features__

__Feature 1__

- __Name: Hustler account __
- __Description: __A youth‑focused transactional and business account designed specifically for Gen Z entrepreneurs, freelancers, and side hustlers\. The account combines everyday banking with a suite of embedded business tools—including instant payment acceptance, micro business loans, and integrated invoicing—so users can manage both their personal finances and their hustle from one place\. Unlike traditional accounts that only store and move money, the Hustler Account enables users to earn income directly through the platform, access working capital tailored to irregular cash flows, and track business performance with simple dashboards\. Whether selling products, offering services, or building a creative brand, users get the tools they need to start, run, and grow their business without needing a separate business bank account\.
- __Value Proposition: __Turns a bank account into a launchpad for earning\. Users can accept payments seamlessly, access flexible credit based on real transaction activity, and gain visibility into their business health—all while keeping costs low and complexity minimal\. It empowers young entrepreneurs to focus on growing their hustle, not navigating banking red tape\.

__Feature 2__

- __Name: Digital wallet __
- __Description: __A specialised digital wallet built for content creators to seamlessly receive, manage, and grow income from their digital content\. The wallet integrates directly with monetisation platforms, allowing creators to track earnings from views, brand deals, and fan support in one place\. An embedded analytics dashboard provides real‑time performance metrics—such as engagement rates, revenue by platform, and audience growth trends—while a built‑in brand partnership marketplace connects creators with relevant sponsors\. Creators earn automatic rewards based on verified engagement: $5 per 100,000 views on eligible content, with higher tier rates for top‑performing creators who consistently drive high engagement\. The wallet also supports easy withdrawals, automated tax‑ready reporting, and tools to reinvest earnings back into content production\.
- __Value Proposition: __Turns creative influence into reliable, manageable income\. Creators get clear visibility into what content pays, effortless reward earnings, and direct access to brand opportunities—all without juggling multiple platforms or chasing payments\. The wallet simplifies monetisation so creators can focus on what they do best: creating\.

__Feature 3__

- __Name: AI Financial assistant __
- __Description: An intelligent, conversational AI companion embedded seamlessly into both the Hustler Account and Digital Wallet\. It provides real‑time financial guidance, automated bookkeeping, cash flow forecasting, and personalised business insights—all through a simple chat interface \(in‑app, WhatsApp, or voice\)\. The AI learns from each user’s transaction behaviour and goals, offering proactive advice to help young entrepreneurs and content creators manage irregular income, track business performance, and make smarter financial decisions\. __
- __Value Proposition: Turns financial complexity into simplicity\. Users no longer need spreadsheets or guesswork—the AI acts as a virtual CFO, automating expense categorisation, flagging tax‑ready insights, predicting cash flow dips, and suggesting when to reinvest or save\. It empowers users to focus on their hustle while the assistant handles the numbers, reduces financial stress, and unlocks better financial outcomes over time\.__

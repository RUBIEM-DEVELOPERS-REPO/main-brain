<a id="header"></a><a id="content"></a><a id="youth-banking-fees-comparison"></a># Youth Banking Fees Comparison

Recent review of official fee schedules shows that most Gen\-Z\-focused accounts have no monthly maintenance charges, except for ZB’s Stash Account which charges a $1 fee[\[1\]](https://www.zb.co.zw/banking/accounts/individual-accounts/stash-account#:~:text=1.%20Initial%20deposit%20,Bill%20payment%20%241)\. CBZ’s CashPlus Teen and FBC’s Scholar account impose no maintenance or transaction fees[\[2\]](https://www.cbz.co.zw/banking/cashplus-teen-account/#:~:text=,p.a)[\[3\]](https://www.fbc.co.zw/banking/sites/default/files/2026-01/250161-FBC%20Bank%20and%20Building%20Society%20Business%20Conditions-%20December%202025.pdf#:~:text=125%20Free%20Banking%20Min%20200,400%20Min%20400%20Max%20500)\. CBZ requires only a $20 minimum balance \(with 2 free ATM withdrawals per month\)[\[4\]](https://www.cbz.co.zw/banking/cashplus-teen-account/#:~:text=,SMS%20Alerts), similar to POSB’s youth account \(initial deposit and fees not explicitly listed\)\. ZB’s Stash account requires no initial deposit and a $2 minimum balance[\[1\]](https://www.zb.co.zw/banking/accounts/individual-accounts/stash-account#:~:text=1.%20Initial%20deposit%20,Bill%20payment%20%241)\. Withdrawal fees differ: POSB charges ~3\.25% per withdrawal \(no free allowance\)[\[5\]](https://www.posb.co.zw/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/CURRENT-BUSINESS-CONDITIONS-AMENDED.pdf#:~:text=ZWG%20128.3%203.25,min%20US%246.50), whereas ZB offers free withdrawals on Stash[\[1\]](https://www.zb.co.zw/banking/accounts/individual-accounts/stash-account#:~:text=1.%20Initial%20deposit%20,Bill%20payment%20%241)\. These details are summarized below from the banks’ official sources \(dates accessed Mar 11, 2026\)\.

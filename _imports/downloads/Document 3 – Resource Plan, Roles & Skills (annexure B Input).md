Document 3: Resource Plan, Roles and Skills
1. Resource Strategy
The   resource   plan   is   designed   to   fully   comply   with   AFC’s   technical   evaluation   criteria,   ensuring:   -
Multidisciplinary coverage - Minimum 10 years’ experience per key role - Demonstrated IDC Core Banking
expertise
Roles are broken down to enable independent costing and aggregation.
2. Proposed Roles and Responsibilities
2.1 Lead Consultant / Engagement Manager
Key   Responsibilities:  -   Overall   engagement   leadership   and   client   liaison   -   Assurance   methodology
oversight - Quality assurance of all deliverables - Executive and Board-level presentations
Required   Skills   and   Experience:  -   Minimum   10   years   banking   IT   and   post-implementation   review
experience   -   Proven   IDC   Core   Banking   platform   expertise   -   Strong   regulatory   and   risk   management
knowledge
2.2 Core Banking Systems Specialist
Key Responsibilities: - Functional and technical review of IDC Core Banking System - Validation of system
configuration and performance - Assessment of benefits realisation
Required Skills and Experience: - 10+ years core banking implementation and review experience - Hands-
on IDC Core Banking system expertise (mandatory)
2.3 IT Architecture and Infrastructure Specialist
Key  Responsibilities:  -   Review   of   system   architecture,   infrastructure,   and   integrations   -   Assessment   of
performance, scalability, and resilience
Required Skills and Experience:  - 10+ years in banking IT architecture and infrastructure - Experience in
core banking hosting environments

2.4 Data Migration and Reconciliation Specialist
Key Responsibilities:  - Validation of data migration accuracy and completeness - Review of reconciliation
and data integrity controls
Required Skills and Experience: - 10+ years in data migration and banking data assurance - Experience in
core banking data conversions
2.5 Information Security and Risk Specialist
Key   Responsibilities:  -   Cybersecurity   and   access   control   assessment   -   Regulatory   compliance   and   risk
evaluation
Required Skills and Experience:  - 10+ years in banking information security and IT risk - Knowledge of
regulatory and supervisory requirements
2.6 Business Process and Change Management Consultant
Key   Responsibilities:  -   User   adoption   and   training   effectiveness   assessment   -   Review   of   change
management practices - Operational readiness evaluation
Required Skills and Experience:  -  10+  years  in  banking  operations  and  change  management  -  Strong
business process optimisation background
3. Compliance with Technical Evaluation Criteria
This resource plan ensures: - At least three (3) senior consultants with 10+ years experience - Coverage of
digital post-implementation, core banking, and operational expertise - Qualifications aligned to Business,
Finance, IT, or Engineering disciplines
Individual   CVs,   certifications,   and   references   can   be   attached   to   meet   Annexure   B
requirements.

## <a id="section-e-product-needs"></a>Section E — Product Needs

1. Which of the following would be most useful to you? \(Select top 3\)

- Dedicated pension account with low or no monthly fees  

- Priority service at branches \(fast\-track queues / dedicated desks\)  

- Assistance with digital banking \(training, guided usage, helpline support\)  

- Funeral cover bundled with account  

- Health/medical cover or access to healthcare financing

- SMS alerts for every transaction  

- Ability to receive pension payments reliably \(no delays\)  

- Family access or joint account management  

- Access to small emergency loans  

- Discounts \(pharmacies, groceries, transport\)

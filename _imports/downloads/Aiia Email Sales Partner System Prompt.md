<a id="email-writing-principles"></a>## Email Writing Principles

1. Sell outcomes, not features
	- Focus on accountability, compliance, efficiency, and national capability
2. Speak the language of power
	- Governance, risk, transparency, national competitiveness
3. Be concise and executive\-level
	- No fluff, no unnecessary detail
4. Create urgency or strategic relevance
5. Position AIIA as inevitable, not optional
6. Frame pilots as low\-risk, high\-impact entry points
7. Use authority, not hype

<a id="email-structure"></a>## Email Structure

Each email should follow this structure:

1. __Subject Line__
	- Strategic and compelling \(not salesy\)
2. __Opening__
	- Demonstrate understanding of the recipient’s institutional challenges
3. __Positioning__
	- Introduce AIIA as the strategic solution
4. __Outcomes / Proof Points__
	- Highlight 1–2 powerful results or impacts
5. __Call\-to\-Action__
	- Soft but confident \(meeting, pilot, discussion\)

<a id="tone-style"></a>## Tone & Style

- Confident

- Government and enterprise appropriate
- Slightly provocative when necessary

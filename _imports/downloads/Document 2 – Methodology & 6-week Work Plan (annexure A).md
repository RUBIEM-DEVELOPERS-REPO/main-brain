Document 2: Methodology and Work Plan
1. Methodological Approach
The proposed methodology is aligned to AFC’s TOR and structured to score  “Above Standard”  under the
technical evaluation criteria. It combines assurance, technical review, and controlled remediation validation.
Phase-Based Methodology
The engagement will be delivered using a five-phase approach:
Phase 1: Initiation and Planning
Project kick-off and stakeholder alignment
Review of project documentation, contracts, SLAs, and regulatory requirements
Finalisation of detailed review plan and evidence request list
Phase 2: Current-State Assessment
System performance, architecture, and infrastructure assessment
Data migration and reconciliation validation
Information security and access controls review
Operating model and vendor performance assessment
Phase 3: Stakeholder Engagement and Validation
Interviews with business, IT, risk, and operations stakeholders
User adoption and change management assessment
Cross-validation of findings and evidence
Phase 4: Reporting and Remediation Planning
Development of PIR report and executive summary
Risk-rating of issues and recommendations
Development of remediation and optimisation roadmap
Phase 5: Implementation Support and Independent Validation
Tracking of agreed remediation actions
Independent validation testing
Formal closure reporting and management sign-off

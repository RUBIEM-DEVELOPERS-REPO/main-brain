WHEREAS the Government of the Republic of Zimbabwe, through the Ministry of Information Communication Technology, Postal and Courier Services \(hereinafter referred to as “the Ministry”\), is mandated to spearhead national ICT policy, digital transformation, and technological innovation;

AND WHEREAS AI <a id="_Hlk200106790"></a>Institute Africa \(AIIA\) is a recognised non\-profit organisation dedicated to the advancement and responsible use of AI for the benefit of Humanity through research, capacity building, innovation, and advisory services;

AND WHEREAS both Parties acknowledge the strategic importance of Artificial Intelligence \(AI\) as a key enabler of socio\-economic transformation, industrialization, and national competitiveness, as articulated in Vision 2030, the National Development Strategy \(NDS2\), and the Zimbabwe National Artificial Intelligence Strategy \(2026–2030\);

WHEREAS the Parties are desirous to establish an AI think tank rooted in Zimbabwe with global influence

AND WHEREAS the Parties have previously engaged in collaborative initiatives including national AI summits and policy engagements aimed at promoting AI adoption in Zimbabwe;

AND FURTHER WHEREAS__ __the parties wish to regularize their partnership in offering AI training, awareness, National AI Policy and Strategy Development 

NOW THEREFORE, the Parties hereby enter into this Memorandum of Understanding \(MoU\) to formalize their cooperation under the terms and conditions set forth herein\.

NOW THEREFORE the parties wish to record that they have reached the following agreement:

# DEFINITIONS

For the purposes of this MoU

- “AI Strategy” means the Zimbabwe National Artificial Intelligence Strategy \(2026–2030\)\.
- “Parties” means the Ministry and AIIA collectively\.
- “Party” means either the Ministry or AIIA individually\.
- “Confidential Information” means any non\-public information disclosed in connection with this MoU\.
- “Programme” means any project, initiative, or activity implemented under this MoU\.

# CONTRACT DURATION AND PERIODIC REVIEWS

- 
	1. This effective date of this MOU shall be the date when the last party appends its signature and shall subsist up to 31 December 2030;
	2.  This MOU shall be reviewed annually and both parties shall be responsible for facilitating regular reviews of this MOU\.
	3. In the event that none of the parties to this MOU give their intention to renew or terminate the MOU, this MOU should renew itself automatically\.

# PURPOSE

The purpose of this MoU is to establish a formal framework for cooperation between the Parties to support the development, implementation, and governance of Artificial Intelligence initiatives in Zimbabwe\.

# THE OBJECTIVES

# ____AI Policy, Strategy and Regulatory Framework____  
Collaborate in shaping, refining, and supporting the implementation of national AI policy, strategy, and regulatory frameworks aligned with Zimbabwe’s Vision 2030, National Development Strategies, SADC, AU priorities, and the National AI Strategy\.

# ____Establishment of a National AI Think Tank____  
Collaborate in the creation and operationalization of a National AI Think Tank to provide strategic thought leadership, policy advisory services, foresight on emerging technologies, and continuous guidance to Government and stakeholders\.

# ____Capacity Building and Skills Development____  
Develop, design, and implement AI training programmes, workshops, executive education, and certification initiatives to build AI skills and human capital across the public sector and broader economy\.

# ____AI Literacy and Awareness____  
Enhance AI awareness and literacy among policymakers, businesses, civil society, and the general public through coordinated outreach programmes, education campaigns, media engagement, and national awareness initiatives\.

# ____Responsible and Inclusive AI Adoption____  
Promote the development and deployment of ethical, transparent, inclusive, and locally relevant AI technologies guided by national values, Ubuntu principles, and international best practices\.

# ____Innovation and Research Collaboration____  
Facilitate research, innovation, and the development of AI solutions, datasets, platforms, and algorithms tailored for Zimbabwe and the broader African region, including commercialization pathways\.

# ____Strategic Public\-Private Partnership Development____  
Promote structured collaboration between Government and private sector AI institutions, drawing from global best practices to co\-design, co\-invest, and implement high\-impact AI programmes and initiatives\.

# ____International Collaboration and Knowledge Exchange____  
Facilitate strategic partnerships with global AI institutions, governments, and development partners to support knowledge transfer, joint research, investment attraction, and Zimbabwe’s positioning as a regional AI leader\.

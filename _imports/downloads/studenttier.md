The student packages for the 2026 AI Summits in Masvingo are designed to make it easier for university and college students, young researchers, start\-up founders, and emerging innovators to participate in Zimbabwe’s artificial intelligence future\. Students can attend either the __AI Tech Forum 2026__, running from __16–17 June 2026__, or the __Zimbabwe 2\.0: AI for National Transformation Summit__, running from __17–18 June 2026__, both at __Urban Lifestyles Hotel in Masvingo__\. The student package options are __USD 300__, which includes summit access, dinner, lunch, accommodation, and transport, and __USD 130__, which includes summit access, dinner, and lunch only\. The USD 300 option is ideal for students who need travel and accommodation support, while the USD 130 option is suitable for students who can arrange their own transport and accommodation\.

AIIA is also reaching out to universities and colleges to support participation by __funding and selecting some of their best students__ to attend these summits\. This creates an opportunity for institutions to expose their top students to practical AI skills, national policy discussions, innovation networks, and future career pathways\. At the same time, individual students are welcome to register independently, provided they can show __proof of enrolment at a university or college__\. The key message is that students should not wait for AI to shape the job market without them — these summits are a chance to learn early, connect with industry and policy leaders, and position themselves for future opportunities\.

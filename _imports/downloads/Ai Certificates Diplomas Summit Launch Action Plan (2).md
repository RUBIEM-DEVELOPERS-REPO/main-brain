<a id="X938d4f6b2d43b8a405218af0b8557f87830b9c7"></a># AI Certificates & Diplomas Summit Launch Plan

<a id="objective"></a>## Objective

Launch the AI Institute Africa Certificates and Diplomas at the 2026 AI Summits as a practical skills pathway for students, professionals, executives, and institutions\.

<a id="launch-concept"></a>## Launch Concept

Position the programmes under one clear brand, for example:

__AIIA AI Academy__  
__Building Practical AI Skills for Zimbabwe’s Future Economy__

The launch should show that AIIA is not only hosting AI conversations, but also creating structured training programmes that help people and organisations adopt AI in practice\.

<a id="proposed-summit-activity"></a>## Proposed Summit Activity

Include a short official launch segment in the summit programme:

- 5 minutes: Opening remarks by AIIA
- 5 minutes: Introduction of certificates and diplomas
- 3 minutes: Announcement of first intake and registration process
- 5 minutes: Photo opportunity and media moment

<a id="programme-categories"></a>## Programme Categories

The certificates and diplomas can be presented in four simple categories:

1. __AI Certificates__ — for students, professionals, entrepreneurs, and managers\.
2. __Executive AI Certificates__ — for CEOs, directors, board members, and senior leaders\.
3. __AI Diplomas__ — for deeper, structured AI training\.
4. __Sector Short Courses__ — for education, finance, insurance, agriculture, public service, media, and SMEs\.

<a id="entertainment-element"></a>## Entertainment Element

To make the launch memorable, include a short entertainment segment immediately after the official launch\.

Recommended options:

- A live band during networking or dinner
- A well\-known local musician for a short performance
- A spoken\-word artist or poet performing a piece on technology, youth, and the future
- A student innovation showcase with music in the background
- A DJ\-led networking session after the launch

Best option: __a short live music or spoken\-word performance after the launch__, followed by networking and photos\. This keeps the event professional while adding energy and memorability\.

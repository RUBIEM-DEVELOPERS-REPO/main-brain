<a id="product-1-afc-pulse-account"></a>## Product 1: AFC Pulse Account

<a id="executive-summary"></a>### 1\. Executive Summary

__Business Model Name:__ AFC Pulse Account  
__Target Sector:__ Gen Z  
__Core Product / Service:__ Digital\-first transactional account designed for Gen Z customers with creator economy and lifestyle integrations\.  
__Primary Customer Segment:__ Youth aged 16–29 including students, creators, freelancers, and first\-job professionals\.  
__Strategic Objective:__ Increase youth banking adoption and build long\-term customer relationships\.  
__Recommended Decision:__ PROCEED

__Strategic Rationale:__ The Gen Z population represents the future core of retail banking\. Capturing customers early in their financial lifecycle allows the bank to benefit from decades of customer lifetime value\. Competitor banks in Zimbabwe are aggressively targeting youth through low\-cost digital accounts\. AFC can differentiate by integrating financial services with income\-generation tools such as creator monetization and youth entrepreneurship support\.

__Key Financials at a Glance__ Year 1 Customers: 10,000  
Year 1 Revenue: USD 230,000  
Break\-even timeline: 24 months

<a id="market-opportunity-analysis"></a>### 2\. Market Opportunity Analysis

__Target Market Definition__ Urban and peri\-urban Gen Z individuals aged 16–29 with access to mobile phones and basic digital services\.

__Market Sizing \(Indicative\)__ Total Addressable Market: 4–5 million youth in Zimbabwe  
Serviceable Market: 1\.5 million digitally connected youth  
Serviceable Obtainable Market \(3 years\): 50,000 customers

__Key Customer Pain Points__ 1\. Difficult onboarding processes 2\. Limited youth\-focused financial tools 3\. Lack of income opportunities 4\. Poor financial literacy

<a id="customer-segmentation"></a>### 3\. Customer Segmentation

__Primary Segment:__ Gen Z students, creators, freelancers, and informal traders\.

__Secondary Segment:__ Parents sending money to students, campus merchants, youth\-led businesses\.

__Customer Persona Example:__ Tatenda, 21, university student receiving financial support from parents\. Uses mobile money daily but wants a bank account that helps manage finances and build credit\.

For Gen Z customers seeking convenient financial services, AFC Pulse provides a digital banking account that enables them to manage money, earn income through creator tools, and access financial education\.

<a id="product-2-afc-hustler-account"></a># Product 2: AFC Hustler Account

<a id="executive-summary-1"></a>### 1\. Executive Summary

__Business Model Name:__ AFC Hustler Account  
__Target Sector:__ Youth Entrepreneurship  
__Core Product / Service:__ Transactional account supporting informal entrepreneurs and side\-hustle businesses\.  
__Primary Customer Segment:__ Young entrepreneurs aged 18–35 running informal businesses\.

__Strategic Objective:__ Capture informal business transaction flows and support youth entrepreneurship\.

__Recommended Decision:__ PROCEED

<a id="strategic-rationale"></a>### Strategic Rationale

A large portion of Zimbabwe’s youth economy operates through informal businesses\. These entrepreneurs require low\-cost transactional accounts, payment acceptance tools, and access to micro\-credit\. The AFC Hustler Account formalizes this segment while creating a new deposit and transaction revenue stream\.

<a id="market-opportunity"></a>### 2\. Market Opportunity

Estimated 2 million informal entrepreneurs in Zimbabwe\.

Target Segment \(initial\): youth entrepreneurs and side hustlers\.

<a id="customer-segmentation-1"></a>### 3\. Customer Segmentation

Primary Segment: \- Online sellers \- Street vendors \- Freelancers \- Service providers

Secondary Segment: \- Supplier networks \- Digital marketplaces

<a id="value-proposition-1"></a>### 4\. Value Proposition

For youth entrepreneurs, AFC Hustler provides a business\-friendly banking account that enables them to receive payments, manage finances, and grow their businesses\.

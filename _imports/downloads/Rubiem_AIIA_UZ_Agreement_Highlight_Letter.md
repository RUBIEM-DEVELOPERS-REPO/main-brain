__RE: CONFIRMATION OF COLLABORATION ARRANGEMENT FOR AFC10/2026__

Dear Sir/Madam,

This letter is issued to highlight and confirm the collaboration arrangement supporting the bid for the Provision of Artificial Intelligence Training Services, Procurement Reference No\. AFC10/2026\.

Rubiem Intelligence is the bidder and lead implementation partner for the assignment\. Rubiem Intelligence will provide strategic digital transformation, banking process, AI implementation, technology architecture, client engagement and programme management capability for the delivery of the AFC Artificial Intelligence and Digital Banking Transformation Programme\.

Artificial Intelligence Institute Africa \(AIIA\) will be responsible for the training delivery component of the programme\. AIIA will lead AI training design, learning pathway development, facilitation, training materials, learning resources, participant engagement, practical exercises, certification coordination and post\-training capability support\.

The University of Zimbabwe will provide academic quality assurance for the programme\. Its role will include academic oversight of curriculum quality, training standards, learning outcomes, assessment integrity and alignment of the programme with recognised academic and professional expectations\.

The collaboration is designed to ensure that AFC receives a technically strong, practically relevant and academically assured programme\. It also ensures clear separation of roles: Rubiem Intelligence as bidder and implementation lead, AIIA as training delivery partner, and the University of Zimbabwe as academic quality assurance partner\.

This arrangement strengthens the proposal by combining implementation experience, structured AI training capability and recognised academic oversight\. It is aligned to AFC's requirement for a training programme that includes quality assurance from a recognised academic institution and supports sustainable capability development across the organisation\.

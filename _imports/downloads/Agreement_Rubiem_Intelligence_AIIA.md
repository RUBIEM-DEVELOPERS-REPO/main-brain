This Collaboration Agreement \(the "Agreement"\) is entered into by and between AI Institute Africa and Rubiem Intelligence, collectively referred to as the "Parties" and individually as a "Party"\.

# 1\. Purpose

This Agreement sets out the binding terms and understanding between the Parties for collaboration in the development, marketing, delivery and support of Artificial Intelligence \(AI\) training programmes\. The purpose of the collaboration is to jointly promote AI education, skills development and capacity building across individuals, organisations and institutions\.

# 2\. Objectives

__• __Design and deliver high\-quality AI training programmes\.

__• __Promote AI literacy, responsible AI adoption and digital transformation capability across Africa\.

__• __Co\-develop curricula, workshops, structured learning pathways and certification programmes\.

__• __Support innovation, applied research and practical AI solutions development\.

# 3\. Scope of Collaboration

## 3\.1 Training Programmes

__• __Joint development and delivery of AI training sessions, workshops, bootcamps and executive programmes\.

__• __Corporate and institutional AI training programmes tailored to client needs\.

## 3\.2 Curriculum Development

__• __Co\-creation of AI training materials, toolkits, case studies and practical resources\.

__• __Development of structured learning pathways, assessments and certification programmes\.

## 3\.3 Events and Outreach

__• __Co\-hosting seminars, webinars, conferences and public awareness initiatives\.

__• __Community engagement and institutional outreach to promote responsible AI adoption\.

## 3\.4 Research and Innovation

__• __Collaboration on AI\-related research projects and practical implementation initiatives\.

__• __Sharing knowledge, tools, methodologies and best practices, subject to confidentiality and intellectual property protections\.

# 4\. Roles and Responsibilities

## 4\.1 AI Institute Africa

__• __Provide expertise in AI education, learning design and curriculum development\.

__• __Facilitate training delivery, learner support and certification processes\.

__• __Support outreach, stakeholder engagement and community capacity\-building initiatives\.

## 4\.2 Rubiem Intelligence

__• __Provide technical expertise, AI implementation experience and digital transformation advisory capability\.

__• __Lead practical training sessions, demonstrations and real\-world application exercises\.

__• __Support business development, client engagement and implementation support\.

## 4\.3 Joint Responsibilities

__• __Jointly agree project plans, delivery schedules, resourcing and client\-facing responsibilities for each engagement\.

__• __Maintain professional standards, quality assurance and ethical conduct in all assignments\.

__• __Protect confidential information and comply with applicable laws and client requirements\.

# 5\. Project Schedules and Work Orders

Specific projects, client assignments, training programmes, deliverables, timelines, fees, revenue sharing, intellectual property treatment and implementation responsibilities shall be documented in written project schedules, statements of work or work orders signed by authorised representatives of both Parties\. Each signed project schedule shall form part of this Agreement\.

# 6\. Financial Arrangements

__• __Financial terms for specific projects or training programmes shall be agreed separately in writing before commencement of work\.

__• __Revenue sharing, cost recovery, invoicing and payment arrangements shall be determined on a case\-by\-case basis and recorded in the relevant project schedule or statement of work\.

__• __Neither Party shall incur costs or bind the other Party financially without prior written agreement\.

# 7\. Confidentiality

Both Parties shall maintain the confidentiality of proprietary, sensitive, commercial, technical, financial, client and operational information shared during the collaboration\. Confidential information shall not be disclosed to third parties without prior written consent, except where disclosure is required by law or by a competent authority\. This obligation shall survive termination of this Agreement\.

# 8\. Intellectual Property

__• __Pre\-existing intellectual property shall remain the sole property of the originating Party\.

__• __Training materials, templates, methodologies, tools or other materials jointly developed under this Agreement shall be jointly owned unless otherwise agreed in writing\.

__• __Client\-specific materials and deliverables shall be governed by the relevant client contract or project schedule\.

__• __Neither Party may use the other Party’s name, logo, proprietary materials or intellectual property outside the agreed collaboration without prior written approval\.

# 9\. Quality Assurance and Compliance

__• __The Parties shall cooperate to ensure that training programmes and advisory services meet agreed quality standards\.

__• __The Parties shall comply with applicable laws, professional standards, client policies and responsible AI principles\.

__• __Where training involves personal data, confidential client data or regulated information, the Parties shall apply appropriate safeguards and access controls\.

# 10\. Term and Termination

This Agreement shall become effective on the date of signing and shall remain valid for a period of ten \(10\) years unless terminated earlier in accordance with this clause\. Either Party may terminate this Agreement by giving thirty \(30\) days written notice to the other Party\.

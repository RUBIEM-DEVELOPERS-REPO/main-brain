__4\.  Value\-Added Services & Features__

__Feature 1__

- __Name: Loan buyout options __
- __Description: AFC can take over a customer’s existing loan from another bank, allowing the customer to consolidate their debt under AFC on better terms\. This helps customers move high\-cost or inconvenient loans into a more affordable and manageable facility\. __
- __Value Proposition: Gives customers immediate financial relief through lower monthly repayments, simplified debt management, and the opportunity to switch to a more customer\-friendly lender\.__

__Feature 2__

- __Name: Mobile ATM__
- __Description: A mobile cash access service that brings ATM or assisted withdrawal services closer to customers through scheduled visits to workplaces, communities, or strategic service points\. This is especially useful in areas with limited branch or ATM access\.__
- __Value Proposition: Improves convenience and accessibility by bringing banking services closer to customers, reducing travel time, queue pressure, and dependence on distant branches or ATMs\.__

__Feature 3__

- __Name: Salary Advance__
- __Description: A short\-term advance facility that allows eligible customers to access a portion of their expected salary before payday for urgent needs\. Repayment is automatically deducted once the salary is credited\.__
- __Value Proposition: Provides quick access to emergency funds without forcing customers into expensive informal borrowing, while giving them a safe and reliable short\-term liquidity solution\.__

__5\.  Key Selling Points__

- Immediate Financial Relief: Converts high\-cost, unmanageable debt into a single, affordable repayment structure, providing instant cash flow relief for customers struggling under existing loan burdens\.
- Unmatched Convenience for Underserved Segments: Brings safe, reliable cash access directly to Pensioners and remote Agricultural communities, eliminating the need for long, costly travel to distant branches or ATMs\.
- Protection Against Informal Lending: Offers a safe, transparent, and controlled alternative to expensive informal borrowing \(loan sharks\) for Civil Servants and salaried School staff who face cash flow gaps before payday\.

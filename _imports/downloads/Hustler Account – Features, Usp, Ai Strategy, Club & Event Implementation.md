# <a id="X4f3c6f292fb48e3415e94638d38124bcd5091c2"></a>HUSTLER ACCOUNT — FEATURES, UNIQUE SELLING POINT, AI STRATEGY, CLUB & EVENT IMPLEMENTATION

# <a id="unique-selling-point-usp"></a>1\. UNIQUE SELLING POINT \(USP\)

## <a id="core-positioning"></a>Core Positioning

__“The Hustler Account is a bank account that pays you to grow your hustle\.”__

Unlike traditional bank accounts that only store and move money, the Hustler Account enables users to: \- Earn income directly through the platform \- Grow their business using embedded tools \- Access financial services tailored to informal income

## <a id="refined-value-proposition"></a>Refined Value Proposition

__A digital\-first youth account that combines banking, income generation, school\-community activation, and AI\-powered business tools — enabling young hustlers to earn, manage, and grow their income within one integrated ecosystem\.__

# <a id="feature-stack"></a>2\. FEATURE STACK

## <a id="a.-core-banking-features-baseline"></a>A\. Core Banking Features \(Baseline\)

These features are essential but not differentiating:

- Multi\-currency account \(USD / ZWL\)
- Send and receive payments
- QR / merchant payments
- Savings pockets
- Basic micro\-loans

## <a id="X0d3317ff1ea2bb29750054ea7e11c8140bfd3f2"></a>B\. Differentiated Features \(Competitive Edge\)

### <a id="creator-monetisation-engine"></a>1\. Creator Monetisation Engine

Users can link a __Creator Wallet__ to their Hustler Account to monetise content\.

__Key Mechanics:__ \- Earn rewards based on engagement \- $5 per 100,000 verified views \(tiered structure\) \- Higher tiers for high\-performing creators

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Turns customers into marketing channels \- Drives organic growth and engagement

### <a id="ai-hustler-assistant"></a>2\. AI Hustler Assistant

An embedded AI tool that supports business growth\.

__Capabilities:__ \- Generate marketing content \(captions, posters\) \- Provide pricing and business advice \- Track income and spending patterns \- Recommend savings and reinvestment actions

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Bridges financial literacy gaps \- Positions AFC as a growth partner, not just a bank

### <a id="smart-income-tracking"></a>3\. Smart Income Tracking

- Automatic categorisation of income and expenses
- Real\-time dashboards \(daily, weekly, monthly\)

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Formalises informal income \- Enables data\-driven credit scoring

### <a id="instant-micro-credit"></a>4\. Instant Micro\-Credit

- Loan eligibility based on transaction behaviour and activity
- Fast approval with minimal paperwork

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Expands loan book \- Uses alternative data for risk assessment

### <a id="hustler-marketplace-future-phase"></a>5\. Hustler Marketplace \(Future Phase\)

- Platform for users to list and sell products/services

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Keeps transactions within AFC ecosystem \- Enables additional fee\-based revenue streams

## <a id="c.-ecosystem-features-growth-drivers"></a>C\. Ecosystem Features \(Growth Drivers\)

### <a id="brand-ambassador-programme"></a>6\. Brand Ambassador Programme

- Influencers promote AFC products
- Earn commissions and bonuses

### <a id="referral-programme"></a>7\. Referral Programme

- Users earn rewards for onboarding new customers

### <a id="low-cost-access-channels"></a>8\. Low\-Cost Access Channels

- WhatsApp banking
- USSD access for low\-data users

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Reduces customer acquisition cost \(CAC\) \- Ensures accessibility across income segments

# <a id="new-service-afc-hustler-clubs-in-schools"></a>3\. NEW SERVICE: AFC HUSTLER CLUBS IN SCHOOLS

## <a id="service-concept"></a>Service Concept

AFC establishes __Hustler Clubs__ in selected schools, colleges, and universities\. Each club operates with an __AFC\-linked club account__ that supports savings, event collections, sponsorship inflows, and club project funding\.

The club becomes a structured platform for: \- Entrepreneurship education \- Startup and side\-hustle showcases \- Savings and business challenges \- Product demonstrations \- Financial literacy training

## <a id="club-account-structure"></a>Club Account Structure

Each AFC Hustler Club will have: \- A registered __AFC club account__ \- Authorized signatories \(e\.g\. patron \+ elected student leaders\) \- Access to club savings and event collections \- Ability to receive sponsorships and club membership contributions

__Strategic Purpose:__ \- Creates early customer relationships inside institutions \- Builds transaction habits before students become full retail customers \- Embeds AFC inside school ecosystems

## <a id="club-activities"></a>Club Activities

### <a id="young-entrepreneur-events"></a>1\. Young Entrepreneur Events

The club hosts recurring events where students present: \- Startups \- Side hustles \- Creative businesses \- Social enterprises

These events can include: \- Pitch sessions \- Product exhibitions \- Pop\-up stalls \- Business competitions

### <a id="featured-guest-participation"></a>2\. Featured Guest Participation

Events include invitations to: \- AFC brand ambassadors \- Successful content creators \- Creator Wallet holders with the highest verified views \- Young business founders \- AFC staff and product teams

### <a id="hustler-challenges"></a>3\. Hustler Challenges

- Best campus startup competition
- Best digital seller challenge
- Most improved hustler award
- Savings challenge linked to Hustler Account usage

## <a id="club-incentive-model"></a>Club Incentive Model

Club members can unlock: \- Priority onboarding to the Hustler Account \- Creator Wallet linkage support \- Event sponsorship opportunities \- Access to AFC business toolkits \- Prizes for highest\-performing student businesses

Top digital wallet holders and creators may receive: \- Event speaking opportunities \- Featured promotion by AFC \- Additional performance bonuses \- Access to pilot services and premium AI tools

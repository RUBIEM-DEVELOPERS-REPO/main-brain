<a id="Xb7ecc6fab51540bdb9975617bd0e8ab50b3cd57"></a># Proposal: Launch of AI Certificates and Diplomas at the 2026 AI Summits

<a id="purpose"></a>## Purpose

This document outlines a proposed approach for launching the AI Certificates and Diplomas during the 2026 AI Summits in Masvingo\. The launch should position the programmes as a national AI skills development pathway for students, professionals, executives, public\-sector leaders, and institutions preparing for the AI\-driven economy\.

<a id="strategic-positioning"></a>## Strategic Positioning

The AI Certificates and Diplomas should not be presented simply as training courses\. They should be launched as part of a broader national skills development initiative that supports Zimbabwe’s digital transformation, workforce readiness, innovation, and responsible AI adoption\.

Suggested positioning statement:

AI Institute Africa is launching a national AI skills pathway designed to equip students, professionals, executives, and institutions with practical AI competencies for the future economy\.

<a id="proposed-launch-theme"></a>## Proposed Launch Theme

__AI Skills for National Transformation__

Alternative names:

- National AI Skills Pathway
- AI Skills Zimbabwe 2026
- AIIA AI Academy
- AI for Workforce and National Development

<a id="launch-opportunity-at-the-summit"></a>## Launch Opportunity at the Summit

The launch should be included as an official segment in the summit programme\. This will give the certificates and diplomas visibility among government officials, industry leaders, universities, students, innovators, and the media\.

Suggested session title:

__Official Launch: AI Certificates and Diplomas for National Skills Development__

Suggested launch flow:

1. Opening remarks by AI Institute Africa\.
2. Presentation of the certificate and diploma pathways\.
3. Remarks from a government, academic, or industry representative\.
4. Announcement of the first intake or founding cohort\.
5. Launch photo opportunity and media coverage\.
6. Registration desk activation for interested participants and institutions\.

<a id="programme-structure"></a>## Programme Structure

The certificates and diplomas can be grouped into clear learning pathways for different audiences\.

<a id="ai-certificate-programmes"></a>### 1\. AI Certificate Programmes

Target audience: students, graduates, professionals, entrepreneurs, civil servants, and managers\.

Purpose: introduce practical AI tools, productivity applications, responsible use, and sector\-specific AI adoption\.

<a id="ai-executive-certificates"></a>### 2\. AI Executive Certificates

Target audience: CEOs, directors, board members, policymakers, executives, and senior managers\.

Purpose: equip leaders to understand AI strategy, governance, risk, policy, productivity, and organisational transformation\.

<a id="ai-diploma-programmes"></a>### 3\. AI Diploma Programmes

Target audience: learners who require deeper and more structured AI training\.

Purpose: build stronger capability in AI systems, data, automation, implementation, ethics, and sector transformation\.

<a id="specialised-short-courses"></a>### 4\. Specialised Short Courses

Target sectors may include:

- Education
- Finance and insurance
- Agriculture
- Public service
- Healthcare
- Media and communications
- Local authorities
- SMEs and start\-ups

<a id="summit-launch-offers"></a>## Summit Launch Offers

To drive immediate interest, the following summit\-only offers can be considered:

1. __Founding Cohort Registration__  
Open applications for the first cohort during the summit\.
2. __Student Launch Offer__  
Provide discounted places for university and college students\.
3. __Corporate Group Package__  
Allow companies and institutions to enrol groups of 5, 10, 20, or more staff members\.
4. __Executive Leadership Package__  
Offer a focused programme for senior leaders attending the national transformation summit\.
5. __Public Sector AI Readiness Package__  
Create a package for ministries, departments, agencies, parastatals, and local authorities\.
6. __Sponsored Student Seats__  
Invite partners and sponsors to fund selected student places\.

<a id="registration-and-sales-approach"></a>## Registration and Sales Approach

A dedicated __AI Skills Registration Desk__ should be set up at the summit venue\.

The desk should allow delegates to:

- Collect brochures
- Scan a QR code to register interest
- Ask about programme options
- Request invoices
- Register for individual enrolment
- Request corporate or institutional training packages
- Discuss student sponsorship opportunities

Recommended staffing:

- One person handling student and individual enquiries
- One person handling corporate and institutional enquiries
- One person capturing registrations and follow\-ups

<a id="media-and-visibility"></a>## Media and Visibility

The launch should be treated as a media moment\. Suggested visibility activities include:

- Official announcement during the summit programme
- Group photo with partners and stakeholders
- Press release to media houses
- Social media launch posts
- Short video clips from the launch session
- Testimonials from students, industry, or government representatives
- Branded backdrop or banner for photos

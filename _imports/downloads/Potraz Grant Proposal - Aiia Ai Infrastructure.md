<a id="potraz-innovation-fund-proposal"></a># POTRAZ INNOVATION FUND PROPOSAL

<a id="project-title"></a>## Project Title

__Development of National AI Infrastructure for Data Readiness, Digital Skills, and Financial Intelligence in Zimbabwe__

<a id="submitted-by"></a>## Submitted By

__AI Institute Africa \(AIIA\)__

<a id="executive-summary"></a># 1\. Executive Summary

AI Institute Africa \(AIIA\) submits this proposal to the Postal and Telecommunications Regulatory Authority of Zimbabwe \(POTRAZ\) for funding under the Innovation Fund to design, develop, and deploy three integrated national AI systems:

- AI Data Readiness System \(ADRS\)
- Cognify – AI Capacity & Learning Platform
- AI Financial Intelligence Platform \(Fintech System\)

These systems form a cohesive national AI infrastructure layer designed to accelerate Zimbabwe’s transition into a data\-driven, AI\-enabled economy, directly supporting Vision 2030 and the Smart Zimbabwe 2030 Master Plan\.

The initiative addresses three critical national gaps: \- Lack of structured, usable datasets \- Limited AI and digital skills capacity \- Low transparency in financial and digital service ecosystems

This project positions POTRAZ as a strategic enabler of national ICT innovation and digital transformation\.

<a id="problem-statement"></a># 2\. Problem Statement

Zimbabwe faces three major systemic ICT challenges:

<a id="data-deficiency"></a>## 2\.1 Data Deficiency

- Fragmented and paper\-based records
- Limited AI\-ready datasets
- Poor interoperability across systems

<a id="skills-gap"></a>## 2\.2 Skills Gap

- Low AI literacy levels
- Limited access to advanced digital training
- Weak pipeline for AI talent development

<a id="financial-opacity"></a>## 2\.3 Financial Opacity

- Hidden costs in banking and telecom services
- Lack of centralized comparison tools
- Limited consumer decision\-making intelligence

These challenges hinder innovation, reduce economic efficiency, and limit the effectiveness of digital transformation initiatives\.

<a id="project-objectives"></a># 3\. Project Objectives

<a id="primary-objective"></a>## Primary Objective

To build a national AI\-enabled digital infrastructure that strengthens data systems, human capacity, and financial intelligence\.

<a id="specific-objectives"></a>## Specific Objectives

1. Develop AI\-ready datasets through ADRS
2. Build AI skills capacity through Cognify
3. Improve financial transparency through AI\-driven platforms
4. Enable innovation through open data and APIs
5. Support regulatory intelligence and ICT sector growth

<a id="adrs-ai-data-readiness-system"></a>## 4\.1 ADRS – AI Data Readiness System

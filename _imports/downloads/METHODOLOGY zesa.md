## 🔷 PROPOSED METHODOLOGY

### ____1\. Approach Overview____

AI Institute Africa, in partnership with Rubiem Intelligence and the University of Zimbabwe, will adopt a __practical, executive\-focused, and impact\-driven training methodology__ designed to ensure immediate applicability within ZESA’s operational environment\.

Our methodology is structured around four key phases:

1. Needs Alignment & Customization 
2. Executive Training Delivery 
3. Practical Application & Demonstrations 
4. Evaluation & Post\-Training Support 

### ____2\. Phase 1: Needs Alignment & Customization____

Prior to the training, we will engage ZESA stakeholders to:

- Align training content with __ZESA’s strategic priorities and operational challenges__ 
- Identify key executive roles and decision\-making contexts 
- Customize case studies relevant to: 
	- Energy generation and distribution 
	- Asset management and predictive maintenance 
	- Customer service and billing systems 

__Output:__

- Tailored training agenda 
- Context\-specific case studies and examples 

### ____3\. Phase 2: Executive Training Delivery \(One\-Day Program\)____

We will deliver a __high\-impact, one\-day in\-person training program__ structured into focused sessions:

#### ____Session 1: AI Fundamentals for Executives____

- Introduction to AI, Machine Learning, and Generative AI 
- Business value of AI in utilities 

#### ____Session 2: AI for Productivity & Decision\-Making____

- AI use cases: 
	- Report writing 
	- Document summarization 
	- Communication enhancement 
	- Decision support systems 

#### ____Session 3: Microsoft 365 Copilot Applications____

- Live demonstrations in: 
	- Word \(report automation\) 
	- Excel \(data insights\) 
	- PowerPoint \(presentation generation\) 
	- Outlook & Teams \(communication efficiency\) 

#### ____Session 4: AI Strategy & Leadership____

- AI governance, risks, and ethics 
- Change management and adoption strategies 
- Building an AI roadmap for ZESA 

### ____4\. Phase 3: Practical Application & Interactive Learning____

To ensure maximum impact, the training will incorporate:

- __Live demonstrations__ of AI tools \(including Copilot\) 
- __Executive case studies__ relevant to the energy sector 
- __Group discussions and scenario\-based exercises__ 
- Hands\-on guided exercises \(where applicable\) 

This ensures participants move beyond theory into __real\-world application__\.

### ____5\. Phase 4: Evaluation & Post\-Training Support____

We will measure effectiveness and support sustainability through:

- Participant feedback and evaluation forms 
- Knowledge assessment during sessions 
- Post\-training report with: 
	- Key insights 
	- Recommendations for AI adoption at ZESA 

Optional:

- Follow\-up advisory sessions for implementation support 

### ____6\. Training Materials and Resources____

Participants will receive:

- Executive\-level slide decks 
- Practical AI use\-case guides 
- Reference materials and toolkits 
- Certificates of completion 

### ____7\. Delivery Method____

- __In\-person training__ \(as required by the tender\) 
- Interactive, facilitator\-led sessions 
- Supported by digital tools and live demonstrations 

### ____8\. Value Addition____

Our partnership brings:

- __Academic rigor__ \(University of Zimbabwe\) 
- __Industry expertise__ \(Rubiem Intelligence\) 
- __Specialized AI training capability__ \(AI Institute Africa\)

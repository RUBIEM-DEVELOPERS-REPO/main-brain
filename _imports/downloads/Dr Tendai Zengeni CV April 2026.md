Dr. TENDAI MASUNDA ZENGENI 
(PhD, MSc, BSc) 
My name is Dr. Tendai Masunda Zengeni and I have extensive experience as a researcher in the field of Optical 
Communications,  telecommunications  and  data  communications  with  11  SCOPUS  International  Journal 
publications as well as serving as a Reviewer in 10 SCOPUS indexed journals and other local journals. With a 
PhD  in  Fiber  Optics  and  Nanophotonics,  which  falls  under  applied  physics,  obtained  from  Universiti  Utara 
Malaysia  and  one  year  of  post-doctoral  research in  High-Speed  Optical  Computing  from  University  of  West 
Attica, Athens, Greece, I have a wealth of knowledge and experience in this field and I am passionate about 
my  work  and  have  dedicated  many  years  to  developing  my  expertise  in  this  area.  Currently,  I am  affiliated 
with Africa University, Mutare, Zimbabwe as a Chairperson at the College of Engineering & Applied Sciences. 
I  was  appointed  as  Board  Vice  Chairperson  for  Netone  cellular,  Zimbabwe  and  I  am  also  a  UNESCO  African 
Women in Tech & AI Fellow  2024, trained at the International Center for Artificial Intelligence – Morocco. I 
am also a  member of the  UNESCO Women for Ethical AI (W4EAI) which  focuses on  AI  Capacity building for 
girls and women. I was also appointed as a UNESCO AI Ethics Expert without Boarders and a Core Team member 
drafting the Zimbabwe AI Strategy. In October 2025 I was appointed Regional Director for Southern Africa at 
Women  In  STEM (Wi-STEM)  Africa and in  April  2026  appointed an  Advisor to the  Zimbabwe  National 
Innovation and Acceleration Centre.

# AI-driven student engagement & rewards

AI‑Driven Student Engagement \& RewardsAI CapabilitiesPersonalised Rewards Engine – Uses transaction and activity data to offer tailored cashback or discounts (e.g., a student who frequently buys art supplies gets offers from local art stores).Club Participation Analytics – Tracks engagement with school clubs (robotics, debate, entrepreneurship) via club wallet activity and event attendance, then suggests relevant skill‑building opportunities or competitions.Gamified Financial Challenges – AI creates micro‑challenges (e.g., “Save 10% of your monthly allowance”) and rewards points that can be redeemed for campus merchant discounts or educational resources.Value to Students \& SchoolsIncreases loyalty and daily engagement with the bank.Positions the bank as a partner in student development, not just a transaction processor.

# NeuroWorks — product identity (guardrail)

NeuroWorks ("The AI Workforce") is RUBIEM's private, local AI-agent platform
powered by clawbot. It is a second-brain knowledge vault plus a multi-persona
task runner — chat, parallel team dispatch, scheduled tasks, pre-organized
teams, and an email bridge — that runs on the user's own machine (loopback,
127.0.0.1).

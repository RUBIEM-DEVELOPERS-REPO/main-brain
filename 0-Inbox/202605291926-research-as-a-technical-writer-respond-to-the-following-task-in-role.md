### 3. Apply a Precise “Technical” Voice  
The Cambridge Dictionary defines *technical* as language that relates to the knowledge, methods, or practical skills of a specific field [1]. Use this guidance to:

* **Use domain‑specific terminology** correctly, but **explain** any jargon on first use.  
* **Maintain consistency** with the platform’s naming conventions (e.g., “NeuroWorks”, “clawbot”, “vault”).  
* **Avoid unnecessary verbosity**; keep sentences concise and action‑oriented.

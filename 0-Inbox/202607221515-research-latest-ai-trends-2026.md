# Research: latest AI trends 2026

Based on the provided evidence, the key AI trends shaping 2026 include:

*   **Agentic AI and Tool Use:** Agentic AI is maturing rapidly, with adoption projected to outpace standard generative AI [1]. These systems combine LLMs with tools to plan, execute, and self-correct in loops [2]. Early 2026 developments include multimodal agent workflows (such as Moonshot AI's Kimi K2.5) and agentic coding models (like Alibaba's Qwen3-Coder-Next) [2].
*   **Reasoning, RLVR, and Efficiency:** AI models are shifting from immediate token generation to "thinking" through intermediate steps before answering [2]. To train these models efficiently at scale, labs are utilizing Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards (RLVR), which bypasses human bottlenecks by automatically checking correctness (e.g., verifying if code runs or math answers are correct) [2]. The focus in 2026 has shifted to *adaptive reasoning*, where models dynamically scale their thinking effort based on prompt complexity to optimize speed and cost [2].
*   **From Copilots to "Vibe Coding":** The enterprise AI ecosystem is expanding with new tool categories, forcing organizations to choose between unified AI platforms and best-of-breed tools [1]. 
*   **Risk Management and Foundational Principles:** Implementing AI risk management programs is becoming a necessity for enterprises, even in jurisdictions without regulatory mandates [1]. Organizations are rewriting their "DNA" by establishing foundational AI principles to align AI deployment with core corporate values and mitigate risks [1].
*   **AI Sovereignty:** AI is balancing between freedom and control as AI sovereignty becomes a top priority for regulators, though global legislative policies remain highly disjointed [1].

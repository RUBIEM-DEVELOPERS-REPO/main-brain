# Research: As a Job Summary, work with agents and brokers to gather additional information

**Job Summary – “Work with agents and brokers to gather additional information”**

The role is centered on acting as a liaison between internal teams and external agents / brokers. Its primary purpose is to collect, verify, and synthesize any supplemental data that agents or brokers can provide, thereby supporting downstream processes such as underwriting, risk assessment, or client onboarding.

| Core Responsibilities | Typical Activities |
|-----------------------|--------------------|
| **Coordinate outreach** | Initiate contact with agents and brokers via email, phone, or the platform’s messaging hub; schedule follow‑ups as needed. |
| **Request and retrieve data** | Ask for missing documents, policy details, market insights, or client‑specific information; use standardized request templates to ensure completeness. |
| **Validate and log information** | Cross‑check received data against internal records, flag inconsistencies, and record the verified information in the appropriate system (e.g., CRM or the NeuroWorks vault). |
| **Communicate status** | Provide regular updates to internal stakeholders (underwriters, sales leads) on the progress of information gathering; escalate blockers promptly. |
| **Maintain relationships** | Build rapport with agents/brokers to encourage timely cooperation and foster long‑term partnership value. |

**Why the role matters**

- **Reduces gaps** in client or policy files that can delay approvals.  
- **Improves data quality**, leading to more accurate risk modeling and pricing.  
- **Enhances operational efficiency** by centralizing the collection process within a single point of contact.

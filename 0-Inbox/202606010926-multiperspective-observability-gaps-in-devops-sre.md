# Multi-perspective research: observability gaps in DevOps/SRE

## Topic statement
Observability gaps in DevOps and Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) refer to mismatches between the data that teams collect (metrics, logs, traces, and SLO measurements) and the information needed to reliably operate, troubleshoot, and improve services. The question is what different bodies of knowledge—best‑practice guides, industry case studies, tooling analyses, and compliance frameworks—identify as the most common gaps, why they arise, and how they are (or are not) addressed.

### 4️⃣ Bridge note (combined vault + web)

**Bridge (card game)**  

- **Objective & play** – Bridge is a four‑player trick‑taking game played in two partnerships. Partners sit opposite each other and aim to win a set number of tricks per hand, scoring points based on contracts.  
- **Bidding** – Most online implementations, including the one at *cardgames.io*, employ the **Standard American Bridge** bidding system, which defines how players communicate hand strength and suit preferences before play. The site even offers optional “Bid Assistance” that explains each bid according to the Standard American Bridge (SAB) convention. [1]  
- **Key online platforms**  
  1. **cardgames.io – Bridge** – Browser‑based, free, with optional bid‑assistance and a simple UI for solo or multiplayer tables. [1]  
  2. **Bridge Base Online (BBO)** – The largest free bridge server, offering unlimited play, ACBL‑sanctioned tournaments, masterpoint tracking, duplicate and IMP scoring, and robust social features (partner matching, chat, clubs). [2]  
- **Community & competition** – BBO hosts a global community where players can join clubs, find partners, and compete for **ACBL masterpoints** through regular tournaments and the annual NABC events. [2]

# Research: review insurance applications and supporting documents job summary

**Job Summary – Insurance Application Reviewer**

The Insurance Application Reviewer is responsible for **examining and evaluating** incoming insurance applications and all accompanying documentation (e.g., identification, medical records, financial statements) to determine eligibility, accuracy, and compliance with company policies and regulatory requirements.  

Key duties include:  

| Duty | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Document Review** | Re‑read each application and supporting files to verify completeness, consistency, and authenticity. |
| **Risk Assessment** | Analyse the applicant’s risk profile (e.g., health status, claims history, credit) and apply underwriting guidelines. |
| **Decision Making** | Make or recommend a decision—approve, request additional information, or deny—based on the analysis. |
| **Communication** | Notify applicants or agents of decisions, required follow‑up, or needed corrections. |
| **Record Keeping** | Log review outcomes and maintain audit‑ready records in the insurer’s system. |
| **Continuous Improvement** | Identify recurring errors or gaps in submissions and suggest process enhancements. |

**Core Competencies**  

- **Analytical thinking** – ability to “think or talk about something again, in order to make changes to it or to make a decision about it” as defined by Cambridge Dictionary’s sense of *review* [1].  
- **Attention to detail** – spotting inconsistencies or missing data.  
- **Knowledge of underwriting rules** – understanding policy terms, legal constraints, and risk criteria.  
- **Clear written communication** – explaining decisions to applicants or internal teams.  

**Typical Metrics**  

- Number of applications processed per day/week.  
- Accuracy rate (percentage of decisions upheld on audit).  
- Turn‑around time from receipt to decision.  

This role ensures that only qualified, properly documented applications move forward, protecting the insurer’s risk exposure while delivering timely service to customers.

### Quick‑start roadmap for a Head of AI
1. **Audit data assets** – map existing product‑design files, sales/ERP logs, sensor streams, and customer interaction records.  
2. **Pilot 3‑month proofs‑of‑concept**  
   - *Product*: generative‑design prototype for a new racket frame.  
   - *Ops*: demand‑forecast model for the next season’s ski inventory.  
   - *CX*: LLM‑powered chatbot integrated with the e‑commerce site.  
   - *Decision*: AI‑augmented KPI dashboard with natural‑language query capability.  
3. **Build cross‑functional AI squads** (product, ops, CX, analytics) that own the end‑to‑end lifecycle—from data collection to model deployment and monitoring.  
4. **Establish governance** – model‑performance monitoring, bias checks, and clear ROI metrics (time‑to‑market, cost‑savings, NPS uplift).  
5. **Scale** – migrate successful pilots to production, embed continuous‑learning pipelines, and create a central “AI Center of Excellence” to share best practices across the organization.

These levers align directly with the recurring research briefings that call for AI‑enabled improvements across products, operations, customer experience, and decision‑making. Implementing them will create measurable value while positioning the company as an AI‑first innovator in its market.

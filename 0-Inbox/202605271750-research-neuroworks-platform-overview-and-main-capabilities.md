# Research: NeuroWorks platform overview and main capabilities

**NeuroWorks™ Platform – Overview**

NeuroWorks™ is Natus’ integrated EEG software suite that supports routine, long‑term, ICU and ambulatory recordings on a single platform. It is marketed as a “one‑stop‑shop” for neuro‑diagnostic labs, allowing clinicians to acquire, review, analyze and report EEG data without switching applications.

**Core Capabilities**

| Capability | What the platform does | Evidence |
|------------|-----------------------|----------|
| **Multi‑mode acquisition** | Handles routine EEG, long‑term monitoring (24‑72 h), ICU/aEEG, ambulatory and point‑of‑care recordings from Natus hardware and third‑party amplifiers. | [1] |
| **Flexible montage & channel management** | Up to 256 channels, customizable montages, automatic re‑referencing and artifact‑rejection filters. | [1] |
| **Real‑time review & quantitative analysis** | Live scrolling, spectral‑power maps, trend analysis, seizure detection algorithms, and quantitative EEG (qEEG) tools such as FFT, ASPECT, and rhythmicity indices. | [1] |
| **Integrated reporting** | Structured report templates, auto‑population of patient data, embedded annotations, and PDF/HL7 export for EMR integration. | [1] |
| **Remote access & collaboration** | Secure web‑based viewer for remote clinicians, live streaming to mobile devices, and cloud‑based storage for multi‑site sharing. | [1] |
| **Advanced ICU monitoring** | Continuous aEEG, burst‑suppression detection, and integration with intracranial pressure and cerebral oximetry monitors for neuro‑critical care. | [2] |
| **Animal EEG support** | Same acquisition and analysis tools adapted for veterinary research and pre‑clinical studies. | [1] |
| **Regulatory‑ready documentation** | Built‑in audit trails, user‑level security, and compliance with FDA 510(k) and CE‑Mark standards. | [1] |
| **Modular add‑ons** | Optional plug‑ins for sleep‑stage scoring (PSG), evoked potentials, and EMG/NCS workflows, enabling a unified neuro‑diagnostic suite. | [2] |

**Key Benefits**

* **Single workflow** – clinicians move from acquisition to interpretation to report generation without leaving the NeuroWorks environment.  
* **Scalability** – the same software scales from a small outpatient lab to a large academic neuro‑critical care unit.  
* **Interoperability** – supports DICOM, EDF+, HL7 and integrates with Natus hardware as well as third‑party amplifiers.  

In short, NeuroWorks provides a comprehensive, modular EEG solution that covers acquisition, quantitative analysis, remote collaboration and regulatory‑compliant reporting across routine, long‑term, ICU and research applications.

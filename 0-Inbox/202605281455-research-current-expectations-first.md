# Research: current expectations first

**Current expectations – the first instalments of *Great Expectations***  

- Charles Dickens’ *Great Expectations* was originally published serially in his weekly periodical **All the Year Round**.  
- The **first two chapters (i & ii)** appeared on **1 December 1860** – the very start of the serial run.  
- Subsequent chapters were released weekly (chapters iii‑iv on 8 December, chapter v on 15 December, etc.), continuing through August 1861 [1].  

Thus, the “current expectations first” refers to the opening of the novel as it was first presented to Victorian readers: the debut of chapters i‑ii on 1 December 1860.

# Research: 150-word press release announcing AI Tech Forum 2026. Date: 12 November 2026. Venue: Hilton Harare. Keynote theme: responsible AI in African public sector. Voice: AI Institute Africa's official voice. Audience: African tech press + public-sector decision makers

**Press Release – Immediate Release**  

*12 November 2026 – Hilton Harare* – The AI Institute Africa is pleased to announce the **AI Tech Forum 2026**, convening African technology journalists and senior public‑sector decision‑makers to explore “**Responsible AI in the African Public Sector**.” The two‑day summit, hosted at the Hilton Harare, will feature a keynote address that frames ethical AI deployment as a catalyst for transparent governance, inclusive service delivery, and sustainable development across the continent.  

Attendees will engage with leading AI researchers, policy architects, and innovators through panel discussions, hands‑on workshops, and a showcase of home‑grown AI solutions addressing health, education, and civic services. The Forum underscores the Institute’s commitment to shaping AI policies that reflect African values and priorities while fostering cross‑border collaboration.  

For media inquiries, please contact:  
AI Institute Africa – Communications Office  
email: press@aiinstitute.africa  

*End*

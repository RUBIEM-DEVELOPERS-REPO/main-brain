# Research: As a Executive Assistant, respond to the following task in role:

Context: Role: Custom
Title: Ask Evie anything
Description: Free-form task framed through the Executive Assistant role. Empty plan — re-plans against the persona system prompt each run

**Executive‑Assistant Response – “Ask Evie Anything”**

Hi [User],

I’m ready to help you get the most out of Evie. As your Executive Assistant, I’ll make sure your query is clear, focused, and framed so Evie can give you a precise, actionable answer.

### How to Phrase Your Question
1. **State the goal** – What outcome are you looking for? (e.g., a decision recommendation, a summary, a draft email).  
2. **Provide context** – Briefly describe any relevant background, constraints, or stakeholders.  
3. **Specify format** – Do you need a bullet list, a short paragraph, a table, or a draft document?

### Example Templates
| Desired Output | Template |
|----------------|----------|
| **Decision recommendation** | “Evie, please recommend whether we should adopt X software, considering our budget of $Y, the team’s skill set, and the rollout timeline.” |
| **Brief summary** | “Summarize the key points from the latest quarterly sales report for the executive team.” |
| **Draft email** | “Draft a polite follow‑up email to the vendor about the pending contract, highlighting our timeline and any open questions.” |
| **Data table** | “Create a table comparing three cloud‑hosting providers on cost, SLA, and data‑privacy compliance.” |

### Quick Checklist Before Sending
- ✅ **Goal** – Clear and specific.  
- ✅ **Context** – Include any numbers, dates, or names that matter.  
- ✅ **Format** – State the preferred output style.  

Once you provide the details using one of the templates (or your own wording), I’ll forward the request to Evie and get the response back to you promptly.

# Research: As a Data Analyst, write findings memos with the chart, the takeaway, and the recommended action

**Findings Memo Template (Data Analyst)**  

*Based on the recurring research items in the vault (e.g., 202605290936‑research‑as‑a‑data‑analyst‑write‑findings‑memos…) the core structure that a Data Analyst should follow is a three‑part memo: a chart, a takeaway, and a recommended action.*  

| Section | What to Include | How to Present |
|---------|----------------|----------------|
| **1️⃣ Chart** | • The most relevant visual (bar, line, scatter, heat‑map, etc.) that directly illustrates the key metric(s) you are analyzing.<br>• Clear axis labels, units, and a concise title.<br>• Source note (e.g., “Data source: Sales DB, Q1‑2026”). | Insert the chart at the top of the memo (or as an attachment) so the reader sees the evidence first. |
| **2️⃣ Takeaway** | • One‑sentence summary of what the chart reveals (trend, outlier, comparison, correlation).<br>• Quantify the insight (e.g., “Revenue grew 12 % YoY; the spike in March accounts for 45 % of the total increase”).<br>• Contextual note if needed (seasonality, data quality caveat). | Place directly under the chart, bolded or highlighted for quick scanning. |
| **3️⃣ Recommended Action** | • A concrete, data‑driven step the business should take (e.g., “Allocate $150 k to the top‑performing product line for Q2”).<br>• Rationale linking the action to the takeaway.<br>• Optional: expected impact or KPI to monitor. | End the memo with a bullet‑point list; keep it actionable and measurable. |

### Example Memo (Illustrative)

```
To: Marketing Lead
From: Data Analyst
Date: 2026‑06‑01
Subject: Declining Email Open Rates – Q1 2026

[Chart: Email Open Rate by Campaign – Jan‑Mar 2026]

Takeaway:
Open rates fell 8 % overall, driven by a 15 % drop in the “New‑Product Launch” series (Feb 2026).

Recommended Action:
- A/B‑test subject lines for the “New‑Product Launch” series within two weeks.  
- Re‑segment the audience to exclude inactive contacts (>6 months no opens).  
- Monitor open‑rate KPI; aim for ≤ 2 % improvement by end of Q2.
```

### How to Use This Template

1. **Gather the data** – pull the latest, clean dataset relevant to the question.  
2. **Create the chart** – use the tool your team prefers (Excel, Tableau, Power BI, etc.).  
3. **Write the takeaway** – focus on *what* the chart tells you, not *why* you think it happened (save that for deeper analysis).  
4. **Define the action** – be specific, time‑bound, and measurable.

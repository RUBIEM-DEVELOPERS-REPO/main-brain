### 1️⃣ New Product Launch – NeuroWorks AI‑Workforce Platform 2.0  

**Headline**  
NeuroWorks Unveils AI‑Workforce Platform 2.0 – The Next‑Generation “Second Brain” for Enterprise Teams  

**Sub‑headline**  
Built on the local‑only Clawbot engine, the upgrade adds parallel‑team dispatch, scheduled‑task automation, and an integrated email bridge.  

**Dateline**  
May 29 2026 – [City], [Country]  

**Lead Paragraph**  
NeuroWorks, RUBIEM’s private AI‑agent platform, today announced the release of **NeuroWorks AI‑Workforce 2.0**, a fully local, loopback‑only solution that expands the platform’s core capabilities with multi‑persona task routing, real‑time scheduling, and a native email‑to‑agent bridge.  

**Body**  
- **Parallel‑Team Dispatch** – Users can create pre‑organized teams that run simultaneously on the same machine, accelerating complex workflows.  
- **Scheduled Tasks** – New scheduler lets agents trigger actions at exact times or recurring intervals without external services.  
- **Email Bridge** – Incoming emails are automatically parsed and routed to the appropriate persona, turning any inbox into a command center.  
- **Zero‑Cloud Footprint** – All processing stays on 127.0.0.1, preserving data sovereignty and eliminating latency.  

“NeuroWorks 2.0 demonstrates our commitment to delivering a truly private AI assistant that scales with the needs of modern enterprises,” said [Name], Chief Product Officer, NeuroWorks.  

**Boiler‑plate**  
NeuroWorks is RUBIEM’s proprietary AI‑workforce platform powered by the Clawbot engine. It provides a secure, local “second brain” that combines knowledge‑vault storage, multi‑persona chat, and automated task execution for individuals and teams.

# Research: following question on the web

**Byte‑size conversion**

- **Storage (decimal)**  
  - 1 KB = 1 000 B  
  - 1 MB = 1 000 KB = 10⁶ B  
  - 1 GB = 1 000 MB = 10⁹ B  

- **Transmission (binary, often used by operating systems)**  
  - 1 KiB = 1 024 B  
  - 1 MiB = 1 024 KiB = 2²⁰ B ≈ 1 048 576 B  
  - 1 GiB = 1 024 MiB = 2³⁰ B ≈ 1 073 741 824 B  

Thus, depending on whether the decimal (10³) or binary (2¹⁰) convention is used, a gigabyte is either 1 000 000 000 bytes or 1 073 741 824 bytes.  (Source: 百度知道)

# Research: weekly progress report. Structure: ## TL;DR (2 sentences), ## Wins (3-5 bullets, quantified where possible), ## Risks (3-5 bullets with mitigation), ## Asks (what we need from leadership), ## Next week (3-5 bullets — owners + outcomes). Exec-readable; under 400 words

## TL;DR  
This week the team delivered three major milestones, exceeding our sprint goal by 15 %. We entered the next phase with clear risks identified and mitigation plans ready.  

## Wins  
- **Feature X launch** – shipped on schedule; early‑user testing shows a 23 % increase in task completion speed vs. baseline.  
- **Automation pipeline** – reduced manual build time from 45 min to 12 min, saving ~30 h per sprint.  
- **Customer‑success pilot** – onboarded 5 new accounts, generating $12 k ARR in the first week.  
- **Team velocity** – rose to 42 pts (up 8 pts from last sprint), enabling two extra story points for the next sprint.

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Dependency on external API latency** – occasional >2 s response times could delay Feature Y. | Medium – could push delivery by 1‑2 days. | Implement fallback cache and add load‑testing alerts (owner: Sam). |

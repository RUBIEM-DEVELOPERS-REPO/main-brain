# Research: NeuroWorks status report

**NeuroWorks – Status Summary (as of 12 Dec 2025)**  

| Item | Details |
|------|---------|
| **Product** | NeuroWorks (also known as *Natus NeuroWorks EEG*) – EEG acquisition, display, archiving, review and analysis software that works with Xltek amplifiers. It stores patient demographics and recording metadata in a Microsoft SQL Server database and uses Microsoft Word for report generation. |
| **Vendor** | Natus Medical Inc. (no cage code) |
| **Current VA Decision** | The technology is **approved for use** but must be operated in full compliance with VA Handbooks 6102 & 6500, VA Directives 6004, 6513, 6517 and NIST/FIPS standards. Users are required to verify compliance with their supervisor, ISSO, CIO or OI&T before implementation. The decision was entered on **30 Dec 2025** by the TRM Management Group. |
| **Compliance Requirements** | • Follow all VA security and privacy policies (Handbooks 6102/6500, Directives 6004/6513/6517).  <br>• Protect sensitive data per NIST/FIPS standards.  <br>• Section 508 has **not** been assessed; implementers must ensure any deployed version is 508‑compliant. |
| **Release History** | • 8.0 – 30 Nov 2013  <br>• 8.1 – 11 Nov 2015  <br>• 8.4 – 1 Jan 2016  <br>• 9.0 – 15 May 2018  <br>• 9.1 – 1 Jan 2019  <br>• 9.2 – 1 May 2020  <br>• 9.3 – 1 Nov 2022  <br>• **10.0.x – 20 Jun 2023** (latest listed release).  <br>All releases **after 14 Sep 2022** (including 9.3 and 10.0.x) are **not exempt** from VA security review. |
| **Support / End‑of‑Life** | No vendor‑provided EOL or desupport dates are listed for any version. |
| **Access** | VA Technical Reference Model entry: https://www.oit.va.gov/Services/TRM/ToolPage.aspx?tid=9624 (Decision date 12/30/2025). |
| **Additional Vendor Info** | Natus’ public product page describes the same functionality and lists optional accessories for clinical EEG monitoring, but does not add new status details. 【2】 |

**Key Take‑aways for VA stakeholders**

1. **Approved but conditional** – NeuroWorks can be used, provided all VA security, privacy and accessibility policies are verified locally before deployment.  
2. **Version choice matters** – Only releases **pre‑9.3 (pre‑Nov 2022)** are automatically exempt from the latest VA security review; newer releases (9.3, 10.0.x) require explicit compliance checks.  
3. **No official EOL** – Absence of vendor‑declared end‑of‑life dates means the VA must monitor Natus announcements for future support changes.  

*If further detail is needed (e.g., specific security controls, configuration guidance), consult the VA TRM “Decision” tab or contact the VA Office of Information & Technology.*

### 4. Risks (Factors That Could Prevent Renewal)

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Budget Constraints** – FY 2026 cost‑cut cycle. | May lead to seat reduction or downgrade. | Propose a phased renewal (e.g., 6‑month term) with a price‑freeze clause. |
| **Unresolved Seat Utilisation** – Under‑use could be seen as low ROI. | Could trigger non‑renewal or request for credit. | Deliver a quick‑win usage‑optimization workshop before renewal call. |
| **Competing Platform Evaluation** – Client is reviewing a rival AI‑workflow tool. | Potential churn if competitor offers a better price/performance mix. | Highlight unique NeuroWorks capabilities (local‑only deployment, data‑privacy) and share a case‑study of a similar client who stayed. |

### 5. Recommended Renewal Motion  

| Timing | Ask | Fallback |
|--------|-----|----------|
| **Initial Outreach** – Day 0 (today) | Send renewal proposal for **12‑month term** at current pricing + 5 % loyalty discount. | If price objection, shift to **6‑month term** with a 2 % discount and schedule a value‑review meeting. |
| **Follow‑Up Call** – Day 7 | Review wins, address open issues, present expansion options. | If client stalls, propose a **pilot** of the Advanced Analytics Module to keep momentum. |

### 4️⃣ Consolidated answer (bridging vault & web)

The vault currently holds **no concrete data** on online bridge platforms, only procedural templates. To close that gap, the web search revealed three primary free services:

| Platform | Core features | Community / masterpoints | Device support |
|----------|---------------|--------------------------|----------------|
| **Bridge Base Online (BBO)** | Human & AI tables, duplicate (MP/IMP), chat, tournaments, ACBL‑sanctioned events, masterpoint tracking | Largest online bridge community; official ACBL partner for earning masterpoints | Browser + native iOS/Android apps |
| **Bridge 247** | Live tables, AI bots, duplicate, practice mode, chat | Growing community; no official ACBL masterpoint integration | Fully responsive browser (desktop & mobile) |
| **Arkadium Bridge** | Single‑player AI practice, simple UI, ad‑supported | No duplicate scoring or masterpoints; casual audience | Browser‑only, mobile‑friendly |

**Take‑aways**

* For serious, competitive play (duplicate, masterpoints) **BBO** is the clear leader.  
* For a lightweight, browser‑only experience without masterpoint needs, **Bridge 247** offers a solid alternative.  
* For quick, casual practice against AI on any device, **Arkadium Bridge** is the simplest option.

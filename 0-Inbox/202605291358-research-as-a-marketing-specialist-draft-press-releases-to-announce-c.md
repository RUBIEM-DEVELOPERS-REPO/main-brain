# Research: As a Marketing Specialist, draft press releases to announce company news

**Press Release – Draft (Marketing Specialist)**  

*[Company Name]* Announces *[Key News Item]*  

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**  
[City, State] – *[Date]*  

**Headline:**  
*Concise, attention‑grabbing statement that conveys the core news (e.g., “[Company] Launches Revolutionary AI‑Powered Platform to Transform Remote Collaboration”).*  

**Sub‑headline (optional):**  
*One‑sentence supporting line that adds context or a compelling benefit.*  

**Lead Paragraph:**  
- Who: *[Company]*, a leader in *[industry/sector]*.  
- What: Announces *[the specific news – product launch, partnership, funding round, acquisition, etc.]*.  
- When: Effective *[date]* or “today”.  
- Where: *[Location or market]*.  
- Why/How: Briefly explain the significance and the problem it solves for customers.  

**Key Details (bulleted or short paragraphs):**  
- **What’s new:** Describe the main features, services, or milestones.  
- **Impact:** Quantify expected benefits (e.g., “expected to increase productivity by 30%” or “will serve over 1 million users”).  
- **Quotes:** Include a statement from a senior executive (CEO, CTO, etc.) that adds credibility and enthusiasm.  
- **Partnerships/Investors (if applicable):** Name any collaborators or backers and their role.  
- **Availability/Next Steps:** Release dates, pricing, beta access, or how to get more information.  

**About *[Company]*:**  
A brief, 2‑3 sentence corporate overview—mission, history, and core competencies.  

**Media Contact:**  
Name – Title  
Phone: *[phone number]*  
Email: *[email address]*  
Website: *[company URL]*  

**###**  

*Note:* This template follows standard press‑release conventions and can be customized for any company announcement. Since the vault contains no specific details about your organization or the news item, you’ll need to insert the appropriate facts, figures, and quotes to complete the release.

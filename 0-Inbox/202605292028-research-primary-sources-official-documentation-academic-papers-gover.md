### How to access the material
1. **Visit the PIDE research hub** – the page aggregates the official statistics, policy PDFs, and dataset download links: https://pide.org.pk/research/primary-education-in-pakistan/  
2. **For the original policy PDFs** – use the Ministry of Federal Education’s document repository (search “National Education Policy 2009 PDF”).  
3. **Constitutional articles & the 18th Amendment** – download the official Gazette PDF from the Government of Pakistan’s legal portal.  
4. **ADB report** – the full PDF is available on the Asian Development Bank’s website (search “Pakistan education sector assessment 2019”).  

These sources together provide the **official documentation, government filings, press releases, and raw datasets** needed for rigorous research on primary education in Pakistan.

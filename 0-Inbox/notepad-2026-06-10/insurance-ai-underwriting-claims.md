# Insurance — AI for underwriting & claims

1. Underwriting supportUse ChatGPT to speed up risk review and documentation.Examples:Summarize client submissions, proposal forms, financial statements, and site reports.Extract key risk factors from PDFs, emails, claims history, or inspection notes.Draft underwriting memos with sections like risk overview, red flags, recommended terms, exclusions, and pricing considerations.Compare a new submission against underwriting guidelines.Generate follow-up questions for brokers or clients when information is missing.Classify risks by industry, size, geography, or exposure type.Example prompt:“Review this client submission and produce an underwriting summary with key exposures, missing information, and recommended follow-up questions.”2. Claims handlingChatGPT can assist claims teams with triage, consistency, and faster communication.Examples:Summarize claim files and timelines.Extract parties involved, incident dates, policy numbers, loss amounts, and supporting documents.Draft claim acknowledgment emails.Identify missing documents needed to assess a claim.Compare claim details against policy wording.Generate settlement rationale summaries.Detect inconsistencies across statements, invoices, photos, and reports.Example prompt:“Summarize this claim file into: facts of loss, coverage issues, documents received, missing documents, and recommended next action.”

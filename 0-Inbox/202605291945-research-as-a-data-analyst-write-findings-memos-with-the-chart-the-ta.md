### How to Use This Template
1. **Populate the “Objective”** with the specific business question you are addressing.  
2. **Gather the relevant data** and calculate the key metrics.  
3. **Create the chart** in your preferred tool (Excel, PowerBI, etc.) and embed it in the memo.  
4. **Write the takeaway** – keep it concise (≤ 1 sentence) and directly tied to the visual.  
5. **Formulate the recommended action** – be specific, actionable, and prioritize.  
6. **Document assumptions** so stakeholders understand the context.  
7. **Distribute** to decision‑makers and schedule a brief walkthrough if needed.

# Research: 150-word press release announcing AI Tech Forum 2026. Date: 12 November 2026. Venue: Hilton Harare. Keynote theme: responsible AI in African public sector. Voice: AI Institute Africa's official voice. Audience: African tech press + public-sector decision makers

**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**  
*12 November 2026 – Hilton Harare*  

The AI Institute Africa proudly announces the **AI Tech Forum 2026**, convening Africa’s leading technology journalists and public‑sector decision‑makers to explore “**Responsible AI in the African Public Sector**.” The two‑day summit, hosted at the Hilton Harare, will feature a keynote address by the Institute’s Director of Policy, outlining practical frameworks for ethical AI deployment across government services, health systems, and education.  

Panel discussions, hands‑on workshops, and a showcase of home‑grown AI solutions will equip policymakers with the tools to harness innovation while safeguarding citizen rights and data sovereignty.  

“Responsible AI is not a luxury—it is a prerequisite for sustainable development in our continent,” said the Institute’s spokesperson. “This Forum will catalyse collaborative action, ensuring African governments lead the world in trustworthy AI.”  

Registration opens 1 December 2026. For media inquiries, contact press@aiinstituteafrica.org.  

*AI Institute Africa – Advancing ethical AI for a thriving Africa.*

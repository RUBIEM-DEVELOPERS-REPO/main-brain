# Research: As a Product Manager, write release notes that name what changed for the customer, not what was shipped

**NeuroWorks – Release Notes (Customer‑Focused)**  

| What the customer experiences | Why it matters |
|------------------------------|----------------|
| **Instant “team‑dispatch” from any chat window** – you can now summon a pre‑configured multi‑persona crew (e.g., research, writing, scheduling) with a single command. | Saves the back‑and‑forth of manually opening separate agents; work starts faster and you stay in the flow of your primary task. |
| **Scheduled‑task engine runs locally** – tasks you set for later (data pulls, report generation, reminder emails) now execute automatically on your machine, even when the UI is closed. | Guarantees that time‑sensitive work happens on schedule without you having to remember or keep the app open. |
| **Unified inbox for AI‑generated replies** – all AI‑drafted messages appear in one view, tagged by the persona that created them. | Reduces the mental load of hunting through multiple windows; you can review, edit, or send replies from a single place. |
| **Version‑controlled knowledge vault** – every change you make to your personal knowledge base is saved with a timestamp and can be rolled back. | Protects your research and prevents accidental loss of valuable context, giving you confidence to experiment. |
| **Local‑only operation (127.0.0.1)** – all processing stays on your computer; no data leaves the machine. | Meets privacy and security requirements for sensitive projects and eliminates latency from cloud calls. |
| **Improved “what‑changed” view** – release notes now list the *effect* on you rather than the technical component (e.g., “You’ll see faster response times” instead of “API v2 added”). | Makes it easy to understand the benefit without parsing internal jargon. |

### How to get the most out of these changes
1. **Try the one‑click dispatch**: type `/dispatch research‑writer` in any chat to see the new team appear instantly.  
2. **Schedule a task**: use `/schedule “run weekly summary” at 09:00` and watch it run even if you close NeuroWorks.  
3. **Check the inbox**: look for the new “AI‑Inbox” tab to manage all AI‑drafted messages in one place.  

These updates are designed to **reduce friction**, **protect your data**, and **make your workflow more predictable**—the core benefits you care about every day.

# Research: As a Product Manager, prioritise with rice or ice — and show the score, not just the verdict.

Context: Role: Custom
Title: Prioritise with RICE or ICE — and show the score, not just the verdict
Description: Persona-derived starter task for "Priya"

**Prioritisation using RICE (or ICE) – with explicit scores**

| Idea / Feature | Reach* | Impact* | Confidence* | Effort* | **RICE Score** (Reach × Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort) | Verdict |
|----------------|--------|---------|--------------|---------|--------------------------------------------------------|---------|
| **A. Self‑service onboarding wizard** | 5 k users / quarter | 3 × (“high” – measurable lift in activation) | 80 % | 20 pt (≈ 2 weeks dev) | (5 000 × 3 × 0.8) ÷ 20 = 600 | **High** – top priority |
| **B. Dark‑mode UI toggle** | 12 k users / quarter | 1 × (“low” – cosmetic) | 90 % | 8 pt (≈ 1 week) | (12 000 × 1 × 0.9) ÷ 8 = 1 350 | **Medium** – quick win, but lower impact |
| **C. AI‑driven task‑suggestion engine** | 3 k users / quarter | 5 × (“very high” – could double productivity) | 60 % | 40 pt (≈ 4 weeks) | (3 000 × 5 × 0.6) ÷ 40 = 225 | **Low** – high effort, lower reach |

\*All values are **relative** estimates (the usual RICE/ICE practice).  
- **Reach** – number of users or sessions that will encounter the change in a given time‑frame.  
- **Impact** – a multiplier (1 = minimal, 2 = moderate, 3 = high, 5 = massive).  
- **Confidence** – % certainty that the Reach and Impact estimates are correct.  
- **Effort** – effort points (1 pt ≈ 0.5 day of a single engineer).  

### How to compute the score
1. **Multiply** Reach × Impact × Confidence (as a decimal).  
2. **Divide** the product by Effort.  
3. **Rank** items by the resulting numeric score – the higher the score, the higher the priority.

### Why showing the score matters
- **Transparency:** Stakeholders see the quantitative basis for the decision, not just a “high/low” label.  
- **Comparability:** Different types of work (UX, infrastructure, AI) can be compared on a common scale.  
- **Re‑evaluation:** When new data arrives (e.g., a pilot test changes Confidence), you can instantly recalc the score.

### Quick ICE alternative (Impact × Confidence ÷ Effort)

| Idea | Impact | Confidence | Effort | **ICE Score** | Verdict |
|------|--------|------------|--------|---------------|---------|
| A – wizard | 3 | 80 % | 20 | (3 × 0.8) ÷ 20 = 0.12 | High |
| B – dark‑mode | 1 | 90 % | 8 | (1 × 0.9) ÷ 8 = 0.1125 | Medium |
| C – AI engine | 5 | 60 % | 40 | (5 × 0.6) ÷ 40 = 0.075 | Low |

Both frameworks lead to the same ordering: **A > B > C**. Use the one that best fits your team’s data‑availability and granularity preferences.

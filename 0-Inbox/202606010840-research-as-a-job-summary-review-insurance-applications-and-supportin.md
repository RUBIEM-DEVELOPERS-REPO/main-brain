# Research: As a Job Summary, review insurance applications and supporting documents

**Job Summary – Review Insurance Applications and Supporting Documents**

| Aspect | Details |
|--------|---------|
| **Primary Purpose** | Examine submitted insurance applications and all accompanying documentation to verify completeness, accuracy, and compliance with company policies and regulatory requirements. |
| **Key Responsibilities** | • Validate applicant information (personal, financial, risk‑related data). <br>• Check that all required forms, signatures, and attachments are present and legible. <br>• Assess supporting evidence (e.g., medical reports, loss histories, property appraisals) for relevance and authenticity. <br>• Identify discrepancies, missing items, or potential fraud indicators and flag them for further review. <br>• Enter or update application data in the underwriting system. <br>• Communicate with agents, brokers, or applicants to request additional information when needed. |
| **Required Skills & Knowledge** | • Strong attention to detail and analytical ability. <br>• Familiarity with insurance terminology, policy types, and underwriting guidelines. <br>• Proficiency in document‑management and data‑entry tools. <br>• Good written and verbal communication for follow‑up requests. |
| **Typical Metrics** | • Percentage of applications processed without error. <br>• Turn‑around time from receipt to initial review. <br>• Number of flagged cases escalated for further investigation. |
| **Reporting Line** | Reports to the **Underwriting Supervisor** or **Claims Operations Manager**, depending on the department’s structure. |
| **Work Environment** | Office‑based (or remote) role with access to the company’s document‑management system and insurance‑application portal. |

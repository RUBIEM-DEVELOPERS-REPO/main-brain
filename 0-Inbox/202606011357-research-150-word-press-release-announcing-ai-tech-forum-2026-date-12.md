# Research: 150-word press release announcing AI Tech Forum 2026. Date: 12 November 2026. Venue: Hilton Harare. Keynote theme: responsible AI in African public sector. Voice: AI Institute Africa's official voice. Audience: African tech press + public-sector decision makers

**Press Release – 12 November 2026**  
*AI Institute Africa*  

Harare – The AI Institute Africa is pleased to announce the **AI Tech Forum 2026**, taking place on 12 November at the Hilton Harare. The Forum will convene leading innovators, policymakers and journalists from across the continent to explore the transformative potential of artificial intelligence in the public sector.  

The keynote address, titled **“Responsible AI in the African Public Sector,”** will set the tone for a day of expert panels, live demonstrations and collaborative workshops focused on ethical governance, data sovereignty and inclusive deployment of AI solutions for government services.  

“We are committed to shaping an AI‑driven future that respects African values and delivers tangible public‑sector benefits,” said the Institute’s Director.  

Members of the African tech press and senior public‑sector decision‑makers are invited to join this landmark event and help chart a responsible AI roadmap for the continent.  

*For accreditation and media enquiries, please contact press@aiinstituteafrica.org.*

# Research: typical SaaS ARR multiples 2025

**Typical SaaS ARR multiples in 2025**

- **Median (50th‑percentile) ARR multiple:** ≈ 6.7 × ARR (SCI value for June 2025)【1】  
- **Common “normal” band:** 6 × – 8 × ARR – this range matches the “Low Old Normal” period of 2016‑2017 and is the prevailing “New Normal” today【1】.  
- **Spread:** The inter‑quartile distance (75th – 25th percentile) has widened to about **6.0** points, meaning the 75th percentile is near **10 × ARR** while the 25th percentile sits around **4 × ARR**. This is a much larger variance than the tighter 3‑point spread seen in the 2016‑2017 low‑normal era【1】.  
- **Tail behavior:** The 90th‑percentile “superstar” multiples remain elevated – the highest‑valued SaaS firms still command **10 × – 12 × ARR** (or more), creating a pronounced right‑hand tail, whereas laggards sit well below the median【1】.

**Take‑away:** In 2025 the “typical” SaaS valuation sits at roughly **6‑8 × ARR**, but expect a wide dispersion: high‑growth leaders can fetch 10 × ARR or more, while slower companies may be valued at 4 × ARR or less. This reflects a “rich‑get‑richer” environment where merely being a SaaS business no longer guarantees a premium multiple.

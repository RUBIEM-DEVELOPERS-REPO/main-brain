## Questions to ask (in order)

1. **Context** – “Can you walk me through how you currently manage cash‑flow forecasting and real‑time reporting?”
2. **Pain** – “Where do you see the biggest inefficiencies or bottlenecks in your current finance stack?”
3. **Cost of pain** – “What impact do those inefficiencies have on your operating margin or compliance costs?”
4. **Trigger** – “What recent events or strategic goals have prompted you to explore new solutions now?”
5. **Decision process** – “How does a technology investment of this scale get evaluated and approved at your company?”
6. **Evaluation criteria** – “Which factors (e.g., cost, integration speed, regulatory compliance) will carry the most weight in your decision?”
7. **Alternatives** – “What other approaches or vendors are you currently considering?”
8. **Success metrics** – “If a solution delivered the outcomes we discuss, how would you measure success in the first 90 days?”
9. **Timeline** – “What is your target timeframe for a proof‑of‑concept or full rollout?”
10. **Budget** – “Assuming a fit, what budget range are you planning for this initiative?”

- **Real‑time insight:** Emphasize dashboards that give instant visibility into liquidity and risk metrics.

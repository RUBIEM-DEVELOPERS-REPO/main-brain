Generally, a purchase requisition is an internal document used to authorize a purchasing department to acquire items or services [1]. In a standard financial workflow, such as those in Dynamics 365 or SAP Concur, a requisition moves through several stages:

*   **Draft:** The requisition for the item (in this case, the $10 bucket) is created but not yet submitted [1].
*   **In Review:** The requisition is submitted to the workflow, where it is routed to the appropriate reviewers or managers for approval [1].
*   **Approved:** Once approved, the internal requisition can be used to generate an external purchase order to submit to a vendor [1]. 

If you need to process or check the status of this specific $10 bucket requisition, you will need to access your organization's active "financeflow" system to see if it is currently in Draft, In Review, or Approved status.

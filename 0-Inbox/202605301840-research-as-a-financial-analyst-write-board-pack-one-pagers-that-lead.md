# Research: As a Financial Analyst, write board-pack one-pagers that lead with the number, then the why

**Board‑Pack One‑Pager Template – “Number → Why”**

| Section | What to Put | Why It Works (the rationale) |
|---------|-------------|------------------------------|
| **Headline KPI** | **[Metric] = X % / $Y bn** (e.g., “Revenue growth = 12 % YoY”) | The headline number instantly tells the board the bottom‑line impact, satisfying their need for a quick, data‑driven snapshot. |
| **Why the Number Matters** | One‑sentence context: market trend, competitive shift, or internal driver that explains the KPI. | Translating the raw figure into business relevance helps directors connect the metric to strategy and risk. |
| **Key Drivers** | • Bullet 1 – major contributor (e.g., “New product launch added $200 m”)  <br>• Bullet 2 – supporting factor (e.g., “Cost‑of‑goods margin improved 30 bps”) | Breaking the headline into concrete causes shows where value is being created or eroded, enabling focused discussion. |
| **Implications & Actions** | • Decision needed (e.g., “Approve $50 m capex for scaling”) <br>• Risk mitigation (e.g., “Monitor FX exposure”) | Explicitly linking the number to required board actions turns data into governance outcomes. |
| **Next‑Step Metrics** | “Target for next quarter: X % growth / $Y bn” | Providing a forward‑looking target keeps the board oriented to progress and accountability. |

---

### How to Use This Template  

1. **Identify the single most important metric** for the agenda (revenue, cash conversion, net‑income margin, etc.).  
2. **Lead the page with that number** in large, bold type.  
3. **Follow immediately with a concise “why”** – a one‑sentence narrative that ties the metric to market dynamics, strategic initiatives, or operational changes.  
4. **Populate the driver bullets** with the top 2‑3 quantifiable contributors.  
5. **State the board’s decision or oversight need** and any risk considerations.  
6. **Close with the next‑step metric** to create a clear performance horizon.

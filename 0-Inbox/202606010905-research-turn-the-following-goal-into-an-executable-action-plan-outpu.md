# Research: Turn the following goal into an executable action plan. Output a numbered list. Each step has: Step / Owner / By when (specific date or N days from today) / Done means (the verification). End with 'Inputs still needed' — list what's unclear that's blocking execution

**Proposed Action‑Plan Template**

| # | Step (what) | Owner | By when | Done means (verification) |
|---|-------------|-------|----------|----------------------------|
| 1 | Clarify the concrete goal (e.g., “launch the new NeuroWorks reporting dashboard”) | Operations Coordinator (you) | **2 days** from today (2026‑06‑03) | Written goal statement stored in `0‑Inbox/goal‑statement.md` and confirmed with the requestor |
| 2 | Identify all required deliverables, success metrics and dependencies | Project Lead (assigned by you) | **5 days** from today (2026‑06‑06) | Deliverables list + KPI table in `project‑specs.md`; dependencies logged in the task tracker |
| 3 | Allocate resources (people, tools, budget) | Resource Manager | **7 days** from today (2026‑06‑08) | Resource allocation sheet approved in the NeuroWorks “Resource” vault folder |
| 4 | Draft a detailed timeline with milestones | Scheduler (Operations Coordinator) | **10 days** from today (2026‑06‑11) | Gantt chart exported to `project‑timeline.md`; milestones dated |
| 5 | Create the first execution sprint (tasks, owners, due dates) | Scrum Master | **12 days** from today (2026‑06‑13) | Sprint backlog in the NeuroWorks “Sprint” board; all tasks assigned |
| 6 | Conduct kickoff meeting and capture meeting minutes | Operations Coordinator | **14 days** from today (2026‑06‑15) | Minutes stored in `kickoff‑meeting.md`; all attendees sign‑off |
| 7 | Execute sprint 1 and track progress daily | Assigned Team Members | **24 days** from today (2026‑06‑25) | Daily stand‑up notes; sprint‑review report showing % completion |
| 8 | Review sprint 1 outcomes, adjust plan if needed | Project Lead & Stakeholders | **27 days** from today (2026‑06‑28) | Updated project‑plan version 2 stored in the vault; change log recorded |
| 9 | Repeat sprint cycles until goal is met | Team | **Ongoing** (each sprint 10 days) | Final “Goal Achieved” sign‑off document and metrics dashboard showing all KPIs met |

---

### Inputs still needed (blocking execution)

1. **The specific goal** you want to achieve (e.g., product launch, research study, internal process improvement).  
2. **Stakeholder list** and their preferred communication channels.  
3. **Budget constraints** or resource limits (people, software licences, hardware).  
4. **Success criteria / KPIs** that will define “Done” for the overall goal.  
5. **Any regulatory or compliance requirements** that may affect planning (e.g., data‑privacy, industry standards).  

*Provide the missing information and the plan can be instantiated directly in the NeuroWorks vault.*

# Research: Turn the following goal into an executable action plan. Output a numbered list. Each step has: Step / Owner / By when (specific date or N days from today) / Done means (the verification). End with 'Inputs still needed' — list what's unclear that's blocking execution

**Executable Action Plan (template)**  

| # | Step | Owner | By when* | Done means (verification) |
|---|------|-------|----------|---------------------------|
| 1 | Clarify the specific goal statement (e.g., “Increase monthly newsletter sign‑ups by 20 %”) | Project Lead | 2 days from today (2026‑06‑01) | Written goal approved in a shared document (e.g., Google Doc) with sign‑off from sponsor |
| 2 | Break the goal into measurable sub‑objectives (e.g., “Create lead‑magnet”, “Launch ad campaign”) | Business Analyst | 5 days from today (2026‑06‑04) | List of sub‑objectives in the project plan, each with a KPI |
| 3 | Assign owners and resources to each sub‑objective | Project Lead | 7 days from today (2026‑06‑06) | Responsibility matrix (RACI) uploaded to the project workspace |
| 4 | Draft a detailed timeline with milestones and deadlines | Scheduler / PM | 10 days from today (2026‑06‑09) | Gantt chart or timeline view in the project tool showing start/end dates |
| 5 | Develop the first deliverable (e.g., design the lead‑magnet) | Assigned Owner (e.g., Marketing Designer) | 20 days from today (2026‑06‑19) | Completed asset stored in the shared drive and reviewed/approved by the owner |
| 6 | Test the deliverable with a pilot audience | QA Lead | 25 days from today (2026‑06‑24) | Pilot results recorded (e.g., conversion rate) and sign‑off email |
| 7 | Launch the full implementation (e.g., run the ad campaign) | Campaign Manager | 30 days from today (2026‑06‑29) | Campaign live URL and spend report captured |
| 8 | Monitor performance against the KPI and report results | Analyst | 40 days from today (2026‑07‑09) | Dashboard showing KPI achievement; final report circulated |
| 9 | Conduct a post‑mortem and document lessons learned | Project Lead | 45 days from today (2026‑07‑14) | Completed post‑mortem document with action items for future projects |

\*“By when” dates are calculated from today (2026‑05‑29) as requested (specific dates or “N days from today”).

---

### Inputs still needed
- **The exact goal** to be achieved (e.g., revenue target, product launch, process improvement).  
- **Stakeholder list** and their authority levels (who can sign‑off each step).  
- **Available budget and resources** (people, tools, budget caps).  
- **Key performance indicators (KPIs)** that define success for the goal.  

*Without these details the template cannot be populated into a concrete, executable plan.*

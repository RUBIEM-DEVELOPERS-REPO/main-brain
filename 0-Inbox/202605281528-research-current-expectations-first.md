- **Publication format:** The novel was originally released **serially** in Charles Dickens’s weekly periodical *All the Year Round*, beginning **1 December 1860** and running weekly through **August 1861**【2†L9-L13】.  

- **Reading‑schedule expectation:** Many modern listeners and readers choose to mirror that original cadence.  The creator of the *Hardcore Literature* channel recommends **receiving two chapters each week** (the first two chapters were published together on 1 December 1860). - **Seasonal context:** Because the opening chapters were published at the start of December, they carry a **Christmas‑time atmosphere**, which the original audience would have experienced during the holiday season【1†L45-L49】.  

- **Current availability:** The first two chapters are now available as a **video narration** (with sound‑scapes and character voices) and will later be released in **audiobook form**【1†L1-L8】.  

In short, the “current expectations” for engaging with *Great Expectations* are to follow the historic weekly release pattern—two chapters per week, roughly 20 minutes of content—while appreciating the seasonal tone of the opening chapters and using the newly‑published narrated video as the entry point.

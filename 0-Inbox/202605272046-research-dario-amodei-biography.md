# Research: Dario Amodei biography

**Dario Amodei – Brief Biography**

- **Birth & Family** – Born in 1983 in San Francisco, California. He is the older brother of Daniela Amodei; his father Riccardo Amodei (an Italian‑American leather craftsman) died when Dario was a young adult, and his mother Elena Engel worked as a project‑manager for libraries [1].

- **Education** – Attended Lowell High School and was a member of the U.S. Physics Olympiad team (2000). Began college at Caltech, transferred to Stanford where he earned a B.S. in physics, then completed a Ph.D. in biophysics at Princeton (thesis on network‑scale electrophysiology) [1]. He later did a post‑doctoral fellowship at Stanford School of Medicine [1].

- **Early Career** – Worked at Baidu (Nov 2014 – Oct 2015) and then at Google before joining OpenAI in 2016 [1].

- **OpenAI** – Rose to become Vice President of Research, overseeing projects such as GPT‑3 [1].

- **Anthropic** – In 2021, Dario and his sister Daniela co‑founded Anthropic, an AI safety‑focused startup that builds the Claude series of large language models. He serves as CEO and is a frequent writer on AI benefits, risks, and policy, advocating an “entente” strategy for democratic nations [1].

- **Public Recognition** – Listed by *Time* magazine among the world’s 100 most influential people in 2025 and again in 2026, also named an “Architect of AI” for *Time*’s Person of the Year [1].

- **Financials** – As of February 2026, Anthropic is valued at roughly $380 billion, and Forbes estimates Amodei’s personal net worth at about $7 billion [1].

- **Key Views** – Argues that AI’s upside and risks are both vastly underestimated; publishes essays such as “Machines of Loving Grace” (Oct 2024) and “The Adolescence of Technology” (Jan 2026) outlining his vision for AI‑driven societal benefit and a taxonomy of AI risks [1].

- **Personal Interests** – Known for outspoken public commentary on AI regulation, job displacement, and geopolitical AI strategy [2].

*Sources: Wikipedia entry on Dario Amodei [1]; Medium profile “The Making Of Anthropic CEO Dario Amodei” [2].*

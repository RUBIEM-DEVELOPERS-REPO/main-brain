The research note records the core responsibility for a Head of AI:

> *“As a Head of AI, partner with leadership, product, engineering, data, legal, and operations teams to bring AI initiatives into production.”* [Vault: 0‑Inbox/202605291356‑research‑as‑a‑head‑of‑ai‑partner‑with‑leadership‑product‑engineering‑…md:10]

In practice, this means the Head of AI must:

1. **Align strategy** with senior leadership to ensure AI projects support overall business goals.  
2. **Collaborate with product managers** to translate AI capabilities into market‑ready features.  
3. **Work closely with engineering** to design scalable, reliable pipelines and integrate models into existing systems.  
4. **Coordinate with data teams** to secure high‑quality data, establish governance, and maintain data pipelines.  
5. **Engage legal/compliance** to address privacy, security, and regulatory requirements before deployment.  
6. **Partner with operations** to set up monitoring, incident response, and continuous‑improvement processes for AI services in production.

These cross‑functional partnerships are essential for moving AI concepts from prototype to live, production‑grade solutions.

# Research: As a Marketing Specialist, draft press releases to announce company news.

Context: Role: Custom
Title: Draft press releases to announce company news
Description: Persona-derived starter task for "AIIA Marketing Specialist v2"

**Press‑Release Draft Template for a Marketing Specialist**  

---

**[Company Logo]**  
**FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE**  
**Date:** [Month Day, Year]  

### **Headline – Clear, Benefit‑Focused, and News‑Driven**  
*Example:* **“[Company] Launches AI‑Powered Customer Insight Platform, Boosting Marketing ROI by 30%”**  

---

#### **Sub‑headline (optional) – Adds Context**  
*Example:* *“New solution combines real‑time analytics with automated campaign orchestration for B2B marketers.”*  

---

#### **Lead Paragraph – The 5 Ws (Who, What, When, Where, Why)**  
- **Who** – *[Company name]*, a leader in *[industry/sector]*.  
- **What** – Introduction of *[product/service/initiative]*.  
- **When** – Launch date or event date.  
- **Where** – Availability (global, specific markets, online).  
- **Why** – The problem it solves and the value to customers (e.g., “enables marketers to target the right audience faster, increasing conversion rates”).  

---

#### **Body Paragraph 1 – Key Details & Benefits**  
Explain the core features and how they align with the **Four Ps of marketing** (product, price, place, promotion) [1]. Highlight measurable benefits (e.g., cost savings, revenue growth, improved brand awareness).  

#### **Body Paragraph 2 – Quotes**  
Include a quote from a senior executive (CEO, CMO) that emphasizes strategic importance, and optionally a customer testimonial.  

> *“This launch reflects our commitment to empowering marketers with data‑driven tools that deliver real business impact,”* said **[Name]**, **[Title]**.  

#### **Body Paragraph 3 – Supporting Information**  
- Market context or research supporting the launch.  
- Partnerships, integrations, or upcoming events (webinars, demos).  
- Any awards or recognitions that reinforce credibility.  

---

#### **Call‑to‑Action (CTA)**  
Direct readers to a landing page, demo sign‑up, or media contact:  

*“To learn more or schedule a live demo, visit **[URL]** or contact **[Name]** at **[email]**.”*  

---

#### **Boilerplate – About the Company**  
A concise paragraph (50‑70 words) describing the company’s mission, history, and core offerings.  

---

#### **Media Contact**  
- **Name**  
- **Title**  
- **Phone**  
- **Email**  

---

### **Tips Informed by Marketing Foundations**  

| Aspect | Guidance (source) |
|--------|-------------------|
| **Message focus** | Emphasize value creation for customers, partners, and society – the AMA definition of marketing [2]. |
| **Promotion mix** | Use integrated channels (press release, email, social, media outreach) as part of the *Promotion* P [1]. |
| **Clarity & Brevity** | Keep the release under 400 words; journalists appreciate concise, news‑worthy copy. |
| **Digital Distribution** | Publish on the company newsroom, distribute via PR distribution services, and share on LinkedIn/Twitter for broader reach [1]. |

---

**[End of Draft]**  

*Feel free to replace placeholders (brackets) with your specific details and adjust the tone to match your brand voice.*

# Research: As a Account Executive, draft follow-up emails that name the next step + a date.

Context: Role: Custom

**Draft follow‑up email templates (next step + date)**  

*Feel free to copy‑paste and adjust the placeholders (e.g., [Client Name], [Product/Service], [Your Name]).*  

---

### 1️⃣ After an introductory call  

Subject: Next steps – [Product/Service] demo on **May 15, 2026**

Hi **[Client Name]**,

Thanks again for the conversation earlier this week. I’m excited about how **[Product/Service]** can help you [brief benefit].

**Next step:** Let’s walk through a live demo so you can see the solution in action.  
**Proposed date & time:** Tuesday, May 15 at 10:00 AM [Time Zone] (or let me know a slot that works better for you).

I’ll send a calendar invite once you confirm. Looking forward to it!

Best,  
**[Your Name]**  
Account Executive – [Company]  
[Phone] | [Email]

---

### 2️⃣ After sending a proposal  

Subject: Review & sign‑off – proposal due **May 22, 2026**

Hi **[Client Name]**,

I hope you had a chance to review the proposal I sent on **May 10**. To keep the timeline on track, the next step is a brief call to address any questions and finalize the agreement.

**Proposed date & time:** Friday, May 22 at 2:00 PM [Time Zone].  

If that works, I’ll set up a short 15‑minute meeting. Otherwise, just suggest an alternative.

Thanks, and I’m here for any clarification you need.

Best regards,  
**[Your Name]**  
Account Executive – [Company]  
[Phone] | [Email]

---

### 3️⃣ After a product trial  

Subject: Trial wrap‑up & next steps – schedule a decision call for **May 29, 2026**

Hi **[Client Name]**,

Your 14‑day trial of **[Product/Service]** ends on **May 27**. I’d love to hear your feedback and discuss the rollout plan.

**Next step:** Decision‑making call to review results and outline implementation.  
**Proposed date & time:** Monday, May 29 at 11:00 AM [Time Zone].

Please let me know if that slot works or suggest another time that fits your schedule.

Looking forward to your thoughts!

Warm regards,  
**[Your Name]**  
Account Executive – [Company]  
[Phone] | [Email]

---

### 4️⃣ After a conference meeting  

Subject: Follow‑up from **[Conference Name]** – schedule a deep‑dive on **June 5, 2026**

Hi **[Client Name]**,

It was great meeting you at **[Conference Name]**. I’d like to schedule a deeper discussion about how **[Product/Service]** can address the challenges you mentioned around [specific pain point].

**Next step:** 30‑minute deep‑dive call.  
**Proposed date & time:** Saturday, June 5 at 9:30 AM [Time Zone].

Let me know if that works or propose an alternative.

Thanks again, and I look forward to continuing the conversation.

Best,  
**[Your Name]**  
Account Executive – [Company]  
[Phone] | [Email]

---

**Tip:** Always include a clear call‑to‑action (confirm the date, suggest alternatives) and a brief reminder of the value you’ll deliver in the next meeting. This keeps momentum and makes it easy for the prospect to respond.

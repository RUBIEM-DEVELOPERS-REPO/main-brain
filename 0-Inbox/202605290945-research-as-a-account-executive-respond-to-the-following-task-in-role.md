**How to Sign‑In or Create a Microsoft Account – Quick Guide for Your Team**

| Step | What to Do | Where on the Page |
|------|------------|-------------------|
| 1. Open the portal | Go to **https://account.microsoft.com/account**. | Home page loads with two clear options: **Sign in** or **Create a Microsoft account**. |
| 2. Sign‑in (existing users) | Click **Sign in**, enter the email/phone and password, then follow any two‑step verification prompts. | Prominent “Sign in” button at the top of the page. |
| 3. Create a new account (new users) | Click **Create a Microsoft account**. Provide a preferred email address (or get a new @outlook.com address), set a strong password, and fill in the required personal details (name, country, birthdate). | “Create a Microsoft account” link/button beside the sign‑in fields. |
| 4. Accept cookies | The site requires cookies to function fully. Make sure your browser allows cookies before proceeding. | A banner at the top of the page notes “To use everything on this website, turn on cookies in your browser settings.” |
| 5. Verify your identity | After submitting the form, you’ll receive a verification code via email or SMS. Enter the code to complete registration. | Standard verification step shown after the form submission. |
| 6. Access services | Once signed in, you can manage your profile, security settings, subscriptions, and all Microsoft services (Office 365, OneDrive, Teams, etc.) from the same dashboard. | Dashboard appears after successful login. |

### Why This Matters for Your Business
- **Single sign‑on**: One Microsoft account unlocks the entire Microsoft 365 suite, simplifying license management for your organization.  
- **Security**: Built‑in two‑factor authentication and cookie‑based session handling protect corporate data.  
- **Scalability**: New users can be added instantly via the same portal, supporting rapid onboarding.

**Bottom line:** Direct your team to the official Microsoft account portal, ensure cookies are enabled, and follow the straightforward sign‑in or registration flow outlined above. This will give every employee immediate, secure access to all Microsoft cloud services.

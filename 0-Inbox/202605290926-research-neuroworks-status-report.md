# Research: NeuroWorks status report

**NeuroWorks – Current Status (May 2026)**  

| Area | What’s happening | Evidence |

### Summary  

- **Cambridge NeuroWorks** is actively expanding its fellowship ecosystem, hosting high‑profile events, and recruiting staff, indicating a growth phase focused on translating neuro‑technology ideas into clinically viable solutions.  
- **NeuroWorks® (Natus)** remains a mature commercial EEG platform with a broad product line, dedicated support services, and ongoing global deployment.  

Overall, NeuroWorks entities are both **operational and expanding**: the Cambridge arm is accelerating research‑to‑impact pipelines, while the Natus arm sustains market presence through software updates and service infrastructure.

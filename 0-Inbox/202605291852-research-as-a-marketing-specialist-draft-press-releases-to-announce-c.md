# Research: As a Marketing Specialist, draft press releases to announce company news

**Press‑Release Drafts for Company News**  
*(All drafts follow the standard news‑wire structure – headline, sub‑headline, dateline, lead paragraph, body, boiler‑plate, and media contact – and incorporate the core “4 Ps” of marketing to ensure clear, compelling messaging.)*  

---

## 1. New Product Launch  

**Headline:** *[Company] Unveils [Product Name], a Game‑Changing Solution for [Target Industry]*  

**Sub‑headline:** *Innovative features and competitive pricing set a new benchmark for [product category].*  

**Dateline:** *City, State – Month Day, Year*  

**Lead paragraph:**  
[Company] announced today the launch of **[Product Name]**, a [brief description – e.g., “cloud‑based analytics platform”] designed to help [primary audience] solve [core problem]. The solution combines **[key benefit #1]**, **[key benefit #2]**, and **[key benefit #3]** at a price point that outperforms existing alternatives.  

**Body (2‑3 paragraphs):**  
- **Product details** – highlight unique features, technology, and how it differs from competitors (the “product” element of the 4 Ps).  
- **Pricing strategy** – explain the pricing model and value proposition (the “price” P).  
- **Distribution/availability** – where and how customers can purchase or access the product (the “place” P).  
- **Promotion** – quote the VP of Marketing on the launch campaign, media outreach, and upcoming webinars (the “promotion” P).  

**Boiler‑plate:**  
*About [Company]* – a brief, AMA‑style description of the company’s mission, history, and market position.  

**Media contact:**  
Name – Title – Phone – Email  

*Sources: definition of marketing and the 4 Ps framework [1]; AMA’s formal marketing definition [2].*  

---

## 2. Strategic Partnership Announcement  

**Headline:** *[Company] Partners with [Partner] to Accelerate [Joint Initiative]*  

**Sub‑headline:** *Collaboration leverages complementary strengths to deliver greater value to [customers/industry].*  

**Dateline:** *City, State – Month Day, Year*  

**Lead paragraph:**  
[Company] and [Partner] today announced a strategic partnership that will combine **[Company]’s** [core capability] with **[Partner]’s** [partner capability] to create [solution/offering]. The alliance aims to expand market reach and enhance the customer experience.  

**Body:**  
- **Purpose & benefits** – describe the joint value proposition (product).  
- **Revenue model** – outline any shared pricing or revenue‑share arrangements (price).  
- **Geographic/Channel reach** – note new markets or distribution channels opened (place).  
- **Joint promotion plan** – co‑branded events, press tours, and digital campaigns (promotion).  

**Boiler‑plate & media contact** – as above.  

---

## 3. Quarterly Financial Results  

**Headline:** *[Company] Reports [Quarter] [Year] Results, Delivering [Key Metric] Growth*  

**Sub‑headline:** *Strong performance driven by [product line] sales and expanded [market] presence.*  

**Dateline:** *City, State – Month Day, Year*  

**Lead paragraph:**  
[Company] today released its financial results for [Quarter] [Year], reporting [revenue figure] (increase of X % YoY) and a net profit of [profit figure]. The growth was powered by [product] and [service] sales, reflecting successful **promotion** and **place** strategies.  

**Body:**  
- **Financial highlights** – tables/quotes from CFO.  
- **Operational highlights** – product launches, market expansion, cost efficiencies.  
- **Outlook** – guidance for next quarter, strategic priorities.  

**Boiler‑plate & media contact** – as above.  

---

### How These Drafts Align with Marketing Fundamentals  

- **Product** – each release clearly defines the offering or news item.  
- **Price** – when relevant (launch, partnership), the pricing rationale is included.  
- **Place** – distribution or market reach is specified.  
- **Promotion** – quotes, media plans, and call‑to‑action embed the promotional mix.  

These templates can be customized for any company news while staying consistent with proven marketing principles [1] and the AMA’s definition of marketing [2].

Context: Role: Custom
Title: Weekly progress report (exec-ready)
Description: Wins, risks, next steps — board-ready sum

## TL;DR  
The team delivered +18 % faster feature rollout, closed 3 critical bugs, and secured $250 k of additional budget. Risks around staffing, data latency, and stakeholder alignment are being actively mitigated, and we need leadership approval for two hires and a pilot budget to sustain momentum.

## Wins  
- **Feature velocity up 18 %** – sprint cycle time fell from 10 days to 8.2 days, enabling the March release two weeks early.  
- **Critical bugs resolved** – 3 high‑severity defects (P1) fixed, eliminating a $75 k potential revenue loss.  
- **Budget increase secured** – finance approved an extra $250 k for Q3 tooling upgrades.  
- **Customer satisfaction rise** – NPS improved from 42 to 48 after the latest UI refresh.  
- **Team engagement** – internal pulse survey shows 87 % of members feel “well‑supported” (up from 73 %).  

## Risks & Mitigations  
| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|------------|
| **Staffing shortage (2 FTE gap)** | Could delay Q3 roadmap | Initiate fast‑track hiring (see Asks) and cross‑train existing members. |
| **Data pipeline latency (+15 % lag)** | Slows analytics for the new dashboard | Deploy interim caching layer; schedule performance review with infra team. |
| **Stakeholder alignment on feature scope** | Scope creep → missed deadlines | Hold a 30‑min alignment sync every Monday; capture decisions in Confluence. |
| **Compliance audit pending** | Potential regulatory hold | Assign compliance lead to complete pre‑audit checklist by 5 May. |
| **Cloud cost overruns** | Budget breach risk | Activate cost‑monitoring alerts; negotiate reserved‑instance pricing. |

## Asks (Leadership Support Needed)  
1. **Approve two senior engineers** (budget $180 k total) to fill the staffing gap.  
2. **Allocate $50 k** for the pilot caching solution to address data latency.  
3. **Endorse the Q3 feature prioritization** document (attached) to lock scope.  

## Next Week (Owners + Outcomes)  
- **Emma (Product Lead)** – Finalize Q3 roadmap & get sign‑off (outcome: approved feature list).  
- **Ravi (Engineering Manager)** – Onboard senior engineers; set up onboarding plan (outcome: hires start 15 May).  
- **Lena (Data Ops)** – Deploy interim caching layer; benchmark latency reduction (outcome: ≤5 % lag).  
- **Sam (Compliance)** – Complete pre‑audit checklist; schedule internal review (outcome: audit ready).  
- **Jia (Finance)** – Update budget tracker with new approvals; communicate to all leads (outcome: transparent spend view).

# Research: As a Job Summary, work with agents and brokers to gather additional information

**Job Summary (Draft)**  

*Title (suggested):* **Agent‑Broker Liaison – Information Gathering**  

*Core Responsibility*  
- Act as the primary point of contact between the company’s internal teams and external **agents and brokers**.  
- **Collect, verify, and consolidate** any additional documentation, data, or clarification that agents or brokers require to process transactions, quotes, or client requests.  

*Key Tasks*  
1. **Initiate contact** with agents/brokers via phone, email, or the platform’s messaging system to request missing or supplemental information.  
2. **Track requests** in a centralized log (e.g., a spreadsheet or CRM) to ensure nothing is overlooked and to provide status updates to stakeholders.  
3. **Validate received data** for completeness and accuracy before forwarding it to the relevant department (underwriting, sales, compliance, etc.).  
4. **Escalate** any unresolved or problematic items to senior staff while maintaining clear communication with the external party.  
5. **Document** all interactions and outcomes in the company’s knowledge vault for future reference and audit trails.  

*Typical Interactions*  
- **Agents** – insurance, real‑estate, or other product‑specific representatives who need client‑level details.  
- **Brokers** – intermediaries who aggregate multiple agents or manage larger portfolios and often request consolidated reports.  

*Desired Skills*  
- Strong written and verbal communication.  
- Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple concurrent requests.  
- Familiarity with the organization’s internal knowledge vault (NeuroWorks platform) for logging and retrieving information.  

*Outcome*  
- Faster turnaround on quotes, applications, or service requests.  
- Reduced back‑and‑forth cycles, leading to higher satisfaction for both agents/brokers and internal teams.
